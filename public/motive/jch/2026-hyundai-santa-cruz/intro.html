The 2026 Hyundai Santa Cruz introduces a bold new approach with its Sport Adventure Vehicle (SAV) design, blending SUV-like comfort with pickup utility. This comprehensive comparison will explore the key differences in performance, utility, technology, and ownership costs, helping Oklahoma drivers make an informed choice between innovation and tradition.
