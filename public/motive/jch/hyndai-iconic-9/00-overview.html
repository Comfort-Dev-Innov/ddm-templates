Overview of the 2026 Hyundai IONIQ 9 and the 2026 Hyundai Santa Cruz


Hyundai's 2026 lineup showcases a brilliant strategy, offering two innovative but fundamentally different solutions to the modern driver's needs. One looks to the horizon, defining the future of family transport, while the other masterfully redefines the utility and comfort available today. This approach ensures that whether a customer is ready to lead the charge into an electric future or is seeking a more versatile version of the present, there is a forward-thinking Hyundai that fits perfectly.

The 2026 IONIQ 9 - The Family SUV, Built in America


The 2026 Hyundai IONIQ 9 arrives as the brand's flagship three-row electric SUV, a statement vehicle designed to compete in the premium segment against formidable rivals like the Kia EV9 and Rivian R1S. It embodies the pinnacle of Hyundai's advanced electrification strategy, promising an unparalleled combination of exceptional range, a luxuriously spacious interior for six or seven passengers, and an array of cutting-edge technology. For the modern Oklahoma family, the IONIQ 9 presents an ideal solution. It caters to those who value sustainability, forward-thinking technology, and the space to grow, all without sacrificing performance or sophisticated style. Assembled in the United States at Hyundai's state-of-the-art facility in Georgia, it also carries the distinction of being a domestically produced vision of the future.

The 2026 Santa Cruz - The Ultimate Sport Adventure Vehicle


In contrast, the 2026 Hyundai Santa Cruz carves its own unique path. It is not a traditional pickup truck but rather a "Sport Adventure Vehicle," ingeniously merging the refined comfort and agile drivability of an SUV—built upon the proven Tucson platform—with the undeniable utility of a compact, open truck bed. This vehicle is a market-segment creator, solving a common dilemma for buyers who find traditional SUVs lack utility and full-size trucks are too cumbersome for daily life.


Its design focuses on urban-friendly dimensions, a versatile four-foot bed perfect for lifestyle gear like bicycles or camping equipment, and a comfortable, well-appointed cabin for up to five passengers. The Santa Cruz is the perfect automotive tool for Oklahomans with active lifestyles. It is equally at home with a homeowner in Yukon tackling weekend projects, an adventurer heading to explore the Wichita Mountains, or a city dweller who needs more flexibility than an SUV can offer without the compromises of a larger truck.
