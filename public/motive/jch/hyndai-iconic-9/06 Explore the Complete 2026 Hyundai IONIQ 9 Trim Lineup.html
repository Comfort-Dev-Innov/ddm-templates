Explore the Complete 2026 Hyundai IONIQ 9 Trim Lineup

The IONIQ 9 lineup spans from a highly efficient base model to a fully-loaded luxury performance vehicle, ensuring a fit for every type of premium EV buyer.

  • S: As the range champion, the S trim is designed for the buyer who prioritizes maximizing miles per charge above all else, offering an impressive EPA-estimated 335 miles of range
  • SE: This trim serves as the smart entry point into the all-wheel-drive lineup. It adds the confidence of HTRAC AWD and comfort features like a power-adjustable passenger seat, making it a well-balanced choice for Oklahoma's varied conditions
  • SEL: Representing the sweet spot for many families, the SEL is the tech and comfort upgrade. It adds highly desirable features like premium H-Tex® seating surfaces, ventilated front seats, a heated steering wheel, and larger 20-inch alloy wheels
  • Performance Limited: This is the gateway to high performance and true luxury. The formidable 422-horsepower dual-motor powertrain becomes standard here, along with an exclusive 6-passenger configuration with second-row captain's chairs, a Bose® premium audio system, and the innovative sliding Universal Island center console
  • Performance Calligraphy: For the buyer seeking the ultimate technology and luxury statement, the Calligraphy trim adds exclusive features like a full-color Head-Up Display and the remarkable Remote Smart Parking Assist 2 system
  • Performance Calligraphy Design: This flagship trim is for the style-conscious driver who desires exclusivity. It offers unique 21-inch turbine-style wheels, exclusive exterior styling, and special paint options like Gravity Gold Matte
