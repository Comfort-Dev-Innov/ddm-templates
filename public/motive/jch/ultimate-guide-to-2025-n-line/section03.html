The 2025 N-Line family offers four distinct models, each tailored to a different type of driver but all sharing the same commitment to style, performance, and value. Here is a detailed look at what makes each N-Line vehicle a compelling choice for drivers in the Oklahoma City area.

The 2025 Hyundai Elantra N-Line
The Agile and Tech-Savvy Sport Compact
The 2025 Hyundai Elantra N-Line is the quintessential sport compact sedan, engineered for those who desire a sharp, responsive vehicle that is as fun to drive as it is efficient for daily commutes.

At the heart of the Elantra N-Line is a spirited 1.6L Turbo GDI 4-cylinder engine that produces a robust 201hp at 6,000 RPM and a substantial 195 lb.-ft. of torque across a wide band from 1,500 to 4,500 RPM. This broad torque curve means that acceleration is readily available from low engine speeds, making the car feel eager and responsive in city traffic.

This energetic engine is paired exclusively with a quick-shifting 7-speed Dual Clutch Transmission (DCT), which features SHIFTRONICÂ® manual shift mode and steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters for drivers who want more control.

KEY UPGRADE
Multi-Link Independent Rear Suspension
The Elantra N-Line's most significant performance upgrade is one that many buyers might overlook: its advanced rear suspension. Unlike the standard Elantra trims, which use a coupled torsion beam axle, the N-Line is equipped with a sophisticated multi-link independent rear suspension.
This is a crucial engineering difference that transforms the vehicle's handling and ride quality. This advanced design allows each rear wheel to react independently to bumps, dips, and imperfections in the road. For you, this translates into a more composed and comfortable ride on Oklahoma's varied road surfaces and, more importantly, significantly improved grip, stability, and predictability when cornering. It is this chassis upgrade, combined with the sport-tuned suspension, that gives the Elantra N-Line its confident, planted feel and makes it so rewarding to drive.
