Finding Your Perfect Powertrain:
A Guide for Oklahoma Drivers

Choosing the right engine is one of the most critical decisions when purchasing a new vehicle. Your daily drive dictates your needs.

Are you looking for maximum fuel efficiency for the daily commute from Norman to Oklahoma City on I-35? Do you need the confident acceleration to merge safely onto the Kilpatrick Turnpike during rush hour? Or perhaps you need the capability to tow a pair of jet skis to Lake Hefner for a weekend of fun?

The 2025 Hyundai Tucson lineup addresses these diverse needs with three distinct powertrain options.
