America's Best Warranty & Complimentary Maintenance

A car is a significant investment, and for many families, the fear of unexpected, costly repairs can be a major source of stress. Hyundai directly addresses this concern with a warranty package that is unparalleled in the industry, effectively creating a financial shield for your family's budget.

10-Year/100,000-Mile Powertrain Limited Warranty

This covers the most critical and expensive components of the vehicle—the engine and transmission—for a full decade or 100,000 miles. For a parent in Guthrie or Midwest City, this means ten years of security against the kind of major repair bill that could disrupt a family's finances.

5-Year/60,000-Mile New Vehicle Limited Warranty

This comprehensive "bumper-to-bumper" warranty covers nearly every other vehicle component, providing peace of mind for the first five years of ownership.

3-Year/36,000-Mile Hyundai Complimentary Maintenance

This program covers normal, factory-scheduled maintenance, including oil changes and tire rotations, for the first three years. This not only encourages proper vehicle care from the very beginning but also eliminates hundreds of dollars in routine service costs, a critical benefit when a family budget is adjusting to new expenses like auto insurance.

Taken together, this package represents a profound commitment to long-term value. It transforms the cost of ownership from a variable, unpredictable expense into a manageable and predictable part of your budget. For a first-time car owner, this is an invaluable advantage that builds confidence and financial responsibility.

Hyundai SmartSense – A Digital Co-Pilot for the Oklahoma City Metro

Driving in the Oklahoma City metropolitan area presents a unique set of challenges. A new driver must learn to navigate a complex web of high-speed interstates like I-40 and I-35, the Turner Turnpike, and the often-congested I-240 loop. They face frequent, long-term construction zones, sudden lane shifts, and interchanges where multiple highways converge, creating confusing and high-stress situations.

Add to this Oklahoma's unpredictable weather—from torrential downpours that reduce visibility to ice storms that create treacherous road conditions—and the need for a vehicle equipped to handle these hazards becomes clear. Statistics show that driver error, particularly related to distraction and excessive speed, is a leading cause of accidents in our area.

Hyundai SmartSense is a comprehensive suite of advanced driver assistance systems that acts as a digital co-pilot, specifically designed to mitigate these real-world risks. It doesn't replace an attentive driver, but it provides a crucial layer of support to help a new driver see more, react faster, and stay safer. Instead of being an abstract list of acronyms, these features provide direct solutions to the daily challenges of driving in Central Oklahoma.

Highway Driving Assist (HDA)

This advanced system is more than just cruise control. It helps keep the vehicle centered in its lane while maintaining a set speed and distance from the car ahead. For a new driver facing the long, mentally taxing commute from Norman to downtown OKC on I-35, HDA significantly reduces fatigue and helps maintain focus, making the drive less stressful and safer.

Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist (BCA)

Changing lanes on the multi-lane I-44 or navigating the complex I-40/I-44 interchange can be intimidating. BCA not only provides a visual and audible warning if a vehicle is in the driver's blind spot but can also apply gentle braking to help avoid a potential collision if the driver attempts to change lanes. It's an essential safeguard for high-volume traffic.

Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian, Cyclist, and Junction-Turning Detection (FCA)

Stop-and-go traffic is a daily reality on roads like the NW Expressway. FCA uses a forward-facing camera and radar to detect potential collisions with cars, pedestrians, or cyclists. It provides a warning and can automatically apply the brakes if the driver doesn't react in time. The advanced Junction-Turning Detection adds another layer of safety at intersections, helping to prevent collisions when making a left turn.
