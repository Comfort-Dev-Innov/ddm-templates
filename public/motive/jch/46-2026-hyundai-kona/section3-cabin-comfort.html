Inside the Cabin: Space and Comfort

Despite their shared platform heritage, the Hyundai Kona elevates passenger comfort with thoughtful design and competitive interior dimensions, especially important for families or groups commuting from one city to another.

  • Front legroom measures 41.7 inches with rear legroom ranging from 36.4 to 38.2 inches, slightly enhancing rear occupant space for adults.
  • The Kia Niro offers respectable passenger room, but its battery pack placement reduces rear cargo flexibility.

The Kona's cargo capacity outshines its competitor with 25.5 cubic feet behind rear seats and expanding to an impressive 63.7 cubic feet with the rear seats folded, more generous than the Niro's 19.4 to 22.8 cubic feet range, depending on model, facilitating large loads for weekend getaways or shopping runs.
