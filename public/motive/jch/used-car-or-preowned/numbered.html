Choosing a Hyundai CPO vehicle gives you a collection of benefits that work together to provide an ownership experience that a typical used car simply can't match.

01

An Industry-Leading Warranty

This is the cornerstone of the Hyundai CPO program. When you buy a CPO Hyundai, you receive:

  • A 10-Year/100,000-Mile Powertrain Limited Warranty: This is reinstated from the vehicle's original in-service date. It covers the heart of your vehicle—critical and expensive components like the engine, transmission, and transaxle. This long-term protection helps you avoid the financial anxiety of a major mechanical failure.
  • The Remainder of the 5-Year/60,000-Mile New Vehicle Limited Warranty: This comprehensive "bumper-to-bumper" coverage protects nearly every other vehicle component, from electrical systems to suspension and steering.
  • For powertrain repairs, there's a minimal $50 deductible per visit, a tiny fraction of what such repairs could otherwise cost.
02

The Meticulous 173-Point Inspection

This isn't a quick check-up. A Hyundai-trained technician invests significant time examining your potential vehicle against a massive checklist provided by Hyundai Motor America. This includes a road test and evaluations of:

  • Mechanical Systems: Engine, transmission, steering, brakes, and exhaust are all put through their paces.
  • Safety Features: Airbags, seatbelts, traction control, and all advanced driver-assistance systems are verified to be fully functional.
  • Interior and Exterior: Everything from the paint and body panels to the infotainment screen and climate controls is checked for quality and proper operation.
03

Guaranteed Quality with Genuine Hyundai Parts

If the inspection reveals any component that doesn't meet Hyundai's standards, it is repaired or replaced. These replacements are made with Genuine Hyundai Parts—the same parts used to build the vehicle at the factory. This ensures perfect fit, function, and longevity, maintaining the vehicle's integrity.

04

Comprehensive Support and Perks

Your protection extends beyond the vehicle itself. A Hyundai CPO purchase includes:

  • 10-Year/Unlimited Mileage Roadside Assistance: Whether you have a flat tire, run out of gas, or lock your keys in the car, help is available 24/7.
  • Rental Car & Travel Interruption Reimbursement: If a covered repair is needed while you're on the road more than 150 miles from home, Hyundai provides reimbursement for rental cars and travel expenses.
  • You get full transparency into the vehicle's past, including service records, ownership history, and confirmation of a clean, non-branded title.
