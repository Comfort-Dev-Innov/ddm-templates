You've decided a CPO Hyundai is right for you. Fantastic! Now, here's how to be an expert buyer and ensure you get the perfect vehicle.

  • Confirm the Certification: Ask to see the paperwork that officially designates the vehicle as a Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned vehicle.
  • Review the 173-Point Inspection Report: Don't just be told it passed; ask to see the signed checklist. This report will show you every single item the technician checked. It's a fantastic testament to the vehicle's condition.
  • Analyze the CARFAX® Vehicle History Report: Look at the service history, the number of previous owners, and confirm there are no reported accidents or title issues like flood or salvage branding. A clean report is a prerequisite for CPO status.
  • Take a Thorough Test Drive: This is your chance to see how the car feels.
    • Drive it to test its performance at different speeds.
    • Listen for any unusual noises from the engine, brakes, or suspension.
    • Test all the electronics: connect your phone, use the navigation, check the backup camera, and play with the sound system.
    • Make sure the heating and air conditioning work quickly and effectively.
  • Understand the Warranty Documents: Get a copy of the CPO warranty booklet. Our team can highlight the key details for you, including what's covered and the term lengths.
