The Pinnacle of Safety: IIHS TOP SAFETY PICK+

Both the 2025 Elantra (for models built after October 2024) and the 2025 Sonata (for models built after November 2024) have earned the highest possible rating from the Insurance Institute for Highway Safety (IIHS): the TOP SAFETY PICK+ award.

Five Stars from the NHTSA

This commitment to safety is further validated by the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration (NHTSA). The 2025 Elantra has received a 5-Star Overall Safety Rating, the agency's top honor. This rating is a composite score based on rigorous frontal crash, side crash, and rollover resistance tests. Historically, the Sonata has also been a consistent recipient of the 5-Star Overall Safety Rating, reinforcing that excellence in safety is a brand-wide priority for Hyundai.
