Performance Tailored for Comfort and Efficiency

Engine and Transmission

Under the hood, the Sentra Base features a 2.0-liter inline-4 engine that produces 149 horsepower and 146 lb-ft of torque. This engine is designed with an aluminum block and head, featuring direct injection to optimize power delivery and efficiency. It is well suited for urban commutes and highway driving around Broken Arrow.

Paired with a continuously variable transmission (CVT), this combination provides smooth acceleration and refined response, with an officially recorded 0-60 mph time of approximately 8.4 seconds—an improvement over previous years and sufficient power for city driving and highway merging without fuss.

Drivetrain and Handling

The Sentra’s front-wheel-drive system delivers predictable handling, ideal for calm, confident drives on Tulsa’s highways and suburban roads. The independent multi-link rear suspension and front strut design balance comfort and stability, providing a compliant ride quality managed for everyday use.

Fuel Efficiency and Economy

The Sentra Base trim's fuel economy is 30 mpg in city driving and 40 mpg on the highway, matching many Oklahoma drivers’ expectations for efficient and reliable transportation. While rivals offer some hybrid options, the Sentra focuses on delivering proven gasoline engine economics without hybrid complexity.
