Why the Mustang Is Special

The Ford Mustang’s allure reaches beyond its specifications, resonating deeply with performance enthusiasts across Oklahoma and the nation. For over half a century, the Mustang has been a symbol of power, innovation, and style.

One reason the Mustang holds a place is its robust lineup of engines. From the efficient 2.3-liter EcoBoost turbocharged four-cylinder producing 315 horsepower to the legendary 5.0-liter Coyote V8 delivering 500 horsepower in models like the Dark Horse™, the Mustang offers a spectrum of power to satisfy diverse drivers in Yukon and Mustang.

The Mustang’s rear-wheel-drive setup, finely tuned suspension, and balanced weight distribution create a driving experience focused on agility and responsiveness. Available features such as the MagneRide® damping system and selectable drive modes allow drivers to adjust handling characteristics to fit any mood or road condition, whether in the urban streets of Edmond or winding country roads outside Moore.

Design-wise, the Mustang retains its classic fastback silhouette with modern aerodynamic enhancements. Inside, a driver-focused cockpit includes a large 13.2" touchscreen, a customizable digital instrument panel, and premium materials that combine retro charm with modern technology.

The Mustang's cultural heritage, performance pedigree, and continuous innovation make it a standout choice for Oklahoma drivers who want a car that's both exciting and practical.
