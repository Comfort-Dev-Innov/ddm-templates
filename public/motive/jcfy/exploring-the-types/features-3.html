Exploring Ford's SUV Lineup

Ford's SUVs cover a broad spectrum, from compact crossovers to full-size family haulers, meeting the diverse needs of Yukon, Bethany, and surrounding areas.

Compact SUVs

Escape and Bronco Sport

The Escape is a well-rounded compact SUV featuring options like all-wheel drive and plug-in hybrid powertrains. It suits everyday driving in Oklahoma City's neighborhoods with a spacious interior and fuel economy.

The Bronco Sport offers enhanced off-road features, including terrain management and rugged suspensions, great for those near Piedmont or Union City who enjoy weekend adventures.

Mid-Size SUVs

Explorer and Edge

The Explorer is a three-row SUV designed for family utility and comfort, with advanced safety technologies and strong powertrains. The Edge, slightly smaller, provides a sporty driving experience with upscale interiors, perfect for drivers around Warr Acres.

Full-Size SUVs

Expedition and Expedition Max

These models are tailored for those needing maximum passenger and cargo space, coupled with impressive towing capabilities. The Expedition Max extends cargo room for long trips across Oklahoma's wide open spaces.
