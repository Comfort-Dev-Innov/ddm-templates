Defining the Breed: Three Distinct Identities

Ford Ranger Raptor

The Agile Trail Athlete

The 2025 Ford Ranger Raptor is engineered for those who seek adventure on tighter, more technical trails where maneuverability is paramount. Its smaller footprint and lighter chassis make it incredibly nimble, dancing through wooded paths and rocky passes that might challenge a larger truck.

Powered by a potent twin-turbo V6, it delivers a rally-inspired driving experience, offering explosive power in a compact and responsive package.

Ford F-150 Raptor

The High-Speed Desert King

The original and the icon. The 2025 F-150 Raptor is the quintessential high-speed desert runner. Its legendary long-travel suspension, anchored by massive FOX™ shocks, allows it to glide over punishing terrain at breathtaking speeds.

The F-150 Raptor combines immense power from its high-output EcoBoost® V6 with a spacious, comfortable cabin, making it the ultimate tool for cross-country adventures, family trips, and serious off-road duty.

Ford F-150 Raptor R

The Apex Predator

This is the most powerful F-150 Raptor ever built. The Raptor R is an exercise in ultimate performance, built for one purpose: to dominate. At its heart lies a monstrous supercharged V8, unleashing a thunderous roar and overwhelming force.

With standard 37-inch tires and a specially tuned suspension, the Raptor R is the apex predator of the off-road world, delivering a visceral, adrenaline-fueled experience unlike any other truck on the planet.
