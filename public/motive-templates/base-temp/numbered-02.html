The Three Divisions of Ford Motor Company

Understanding Ford's organizational structure provides insight into how innovation and production are managed, benefiting customers from Yukon to Moore.

01

Ford Blue

Traditional Vehicles

Ford Blue focuses on internal combustion engine vehicles, including trucks and SUVs that have been staples in communities around Oklahoma City and Edmond.

02

Ford Model E

Electric Vehicles

Dedicated to electric vehicle development, Ford Model E leads Ford's charge in EV innovation with models like the Mustang Mach-E and F-150 Lightning, designed to meet growing demand in areas like Yukon and Piedmont.

03

Ford Pro

Commercial and Service Division

Ford Pro handles commercial vehicle distribution and related services, ensuring that businesses in Mustang and Bethany have access to reliable work trucks and vans, along with comprehensive support.
