Coverage and Maintenance Benefits

Ford provides assurance with the F-150 Lightning, offering the following warranties:

2025 F-150 Lightning Warranty Coverage

Warranty Component Coverage Duration/Distance
Basic Limited Warranty 3 years / 36,000 miles
Powertrain Warranty 5 years / 60,000 miles
Battery and EV Components 8 years / 100,000 miles

Though no complimentary scheduled maintenance is included, Bowen Scarff Ford's skilled technicians specialize in electric vehicle servicing, ensuring your F-150 Lightning receives expert attention throughout its lifespan. For unexpected assistance, Ford Roadside Assistance is available exclusively at Ford Roadside Assistance: 1-800-241-3673, offering manufacturer-backed support anywhere in Washington State.
