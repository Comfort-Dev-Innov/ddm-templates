Ford Certified Pre-Owned Program Rules and Eligibility

The Ford Blue Advantage program is designed with clear, strict rules to maintain high standards and customer trust. Understanding these rules is essential when evaluating Ford CPO vehicles in the Kent, Washington area.

Gold Certified

Gold Certified

For a vehicle to be Gold Certified, it must meet the following criteria:

  • Be a Ford model not older than six years
  • Have logged fewer than 80,000 miles
  • Successfully pass a rigorous 172-point inspection conducted by Ford-trained technicians

To protect your investment, eligible vehicles come with extensive coverage, including a 12-month/12,000-mile comprehensive limited warranty and long-term powertrain protection for up to 7 years or 100,000 miles. This level of certification also grants the buyer a 14-day/1,000-mile satisfaction guarantee and eligibility for FordPass‚Ñ¢ Rewards.

Blue Certified

Blue Certified

Blue Certified vehicles follow a more lenient but still demanding standard:

  • Less than 10 years old
  • Fewer than 150,000 miles driven
  • Pass a 139-point inspection

Blue Certified vehicles come with a 90-day/4,000-mile limited warranty and the same 14-day return option, roadside assistance, and reward points, though on a smaller scale compared to Gold Certification.

While the Blue Certified program extends eligibility to non-Ford makes‚Äîbringing competitive brands into the fold‚Äîthese vehicles operate under a separate tier and do not carry the full suite of Gold Certified warranty benefits.
