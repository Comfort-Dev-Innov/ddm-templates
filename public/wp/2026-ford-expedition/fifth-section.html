Interior Space, Comfort & Luxury

Space, Comfort, and Luxury Inside

Compare how the 2026 Ford Expedition and 2026 Chevrolet Suburban use their generous dimensions, seating layouts, and premium materials to serve families around Kent, Auburn, and beyond.

Seating Layouts & Cargo Capacities

Flexible Seating vs. Maximum Raw Cargo Volume

Expedition

Versatile Seating & Smart Cargo Tunnels

The Ford Expedition provides seating for seven to eight passengers, as well as versatile configurations featuring captain’s chairs or a 40/20/40 split bench in the second row. The third row includes an innovative fold‑flat middle seat that creates a tunnel for long cargo through the cabin— perfect for transporting skis or lumber without sacrificing passenger space. Cargo volume behind the front seats in the Expedition Max model reaches an ample 123.1 cubic feet.

Suburban

Sheer Volume & Nine‑Seat Potential

Conversely, the Suburban leads in sheer cargo volume, boasting up to 144.5 cubic feet behind the first row and slightly more passenger space overall. It also offers up to nine‑seat configurations with the optional front bench. However, the Expedition’s more intelligent seat‑folding and cargo‑management solutions give it an edge in usability and adaptability for diverse daily driving needs across Kent and beyond.

Materials & Cabin Quality

Modern Digital Lounge vs. Traditional Luxury

Expedition

Tech‑Forward, Thoughtful Cabin

Ford’s redesigned dashboard is finished in premium materials, featuring a sweeping 24‑inch digital instrumentation display poised near the windshield, coupled with a 13.2‑inch infotainment touchscreen, merging technology and style impressively. The center console slides rearward, easing second‑row access— showcasing thoughtfulness in design that enhances passenger comfort and convenience.

Suburban

Upscale but More Traditional Layout

While the Suburban offers generous interior upgrades with leather upholstery, wood accents, and large 17.7‑inch infotainment screens, some reviewers consider its cabin layout more traditional and less intuitive by comparison. The Expedition's advanced digital displays and ergonomic innovations elevate driver and passenger experiences, especially during longer family outings or commutes around cities like Auburn or Renton.
