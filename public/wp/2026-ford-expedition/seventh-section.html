Safety and Driver Assistance Innovations

Safety remains paramount for Bowen Scarff Ford customers in Kent and surrounding areas, and the Expedition delivers extensively on this front. Standard features include automatic emergency braking, lane-keeping assist, adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring with trailer coverage, and a comprehensive 360-degree camera system. Exclusive to Ford is the BlueCruise hands-free driving system, offering natural lane-keeping and lane-change assistance with a no-cost trial period—an advanced system not currently matched by Chevrolet’s Super Cruise technology, which comes as a costly option on select trims.

The Expedition also benefits from Intersection Assist and Trail Turn Assist, addressing urban and trail hazards with precision. These features, combined with Ford’s comprehensive roadside assistance (Ford Roadside Assistance: 800-241-3673), ensure peace of mind, especially for drivers often traveling local highways and rural roads near Maple Valley or Auburn.
