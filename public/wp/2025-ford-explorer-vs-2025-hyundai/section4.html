Exterior Styling and Presence Comparison

Compare the design, dimensions, and visual appeal of both three-row SUVs.

2025 Ford Explorer 2025 Honda Pilot
Ford Design

2025 Ford Explorer

The 2025 Explorer's exterior presents a fresh yet familiar face with sculpted lines and four unique grille styles differentiating trims, highlighted by LED headlamps that illuminate the road with clarity. Wheel sizes ranging from 18 to 21 inches add to a purposeful stance, especially with available black roof options and new colors like Vapor Blue Metallic. The Explorer combines muscular proportions with refined finishing touches that turn heads throughout Kent and Auburn alike.

Honda Design

2025 Honda Pilot

In contrast, the Honda Pilot adopts smoother, less aggressive styling cues, with a broad grille and larger overall footprint. The TrailSport and Black Edition trims inject darkened elements and 20-inch wheels, appealing to off-road and premium buyers, respectively. Still, the overall design remains utilitarian compared to the sportier, SUV-focused explorer.
