Technology and Connectivity Features Faceoff

Technology is a hallmark where the 2025 Explorer distinctly outshines the Pilot. The Explorer's Ford Digital Experience infotainment system is built on an Android Automotive platform with a sizable 13.2-inch touchscreen paired with a separate 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster. Features include:
  • Fully integrated Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and smartphone Siri voice control support
  • Fast wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
  • 5G Wi-Fi hotspot capable of connecting 10 devices simultaneously
  • Eight USB ports scattered across all rows, plus multiple 12V outlets
  • Wireless charging pads and media streaming apps such as Prime Video and Peacock
  • Bluetooth gamepad compatibility with preloaded games â€” unique for family entertainment
Honda Pilot tech, while competent, leans on a smaller 7- to 9-inch touchscreen arrangement depending on trim and does not match Explorer's app integration or digital display sizes. Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto appear only on higher trims, and the infotainment experience is less immersive.
