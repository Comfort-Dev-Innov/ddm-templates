Power and Performance for the Pacific Northwest

Whether you're hauling equipment, delivering goods, or transporting a crew, the 2025 Ford Transit offers powertrain options designed to handle the diverse demands of the Puget Sound region. Ford continues its tradition of providing robust and capable engines that strike a balance between power and efficiency.

3.5-Liter V6 (Standard)
The standard engine is the proven 3.5-liter V6, delivering ample horsepower and torque for daily tasks.
3.5-Liter EcoBoost V6
For those requiring more muscle to tackle steeper grades or heavier loads, the available 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 engine is the ideal choice. This twin-turbocharged powerhouse delivers exceptional performance without compromising fuel economy, making it a wise investment for businesses seeking to optimize their operational costs.

Both engines are paired with a smooth and responsive 10-speed automatic transmission, ensuring confident acceleration and efficient power delivery. An intelligent all-wheel-drive (AWD) system is also available, providing enhanced traction and control during Washington's notoriously rainy seasons.
