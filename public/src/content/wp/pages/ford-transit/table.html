Unmatched Versatility: Configured for Your Kent Business

The true genius of the Ford Transit lies in its incredible versatility. No two businesses are the same, and the Transit can be configured in a seemingly endless number of ways to meet your specific needs. From florists at Pike Place Market to construction crews in Renton, there is a Transit for every trade.

Key Configurations Include:

  • Roof Heights: Choose from low, medium, and high roof options, allowing for easier access to parking garages or incredible stand-up interior space for a mobile workshop.
  • Wheelbases: Multiple wheelbase lengths are available, directly impacting cargo volume and maneuverability.
  • Body Styles: The Transit is available in various body styles, including a Cargo Van, a Passenger Van (also known as a Wagon), a Crew Van, a Cutaway, and a Chassis Cab, providing a foundational platform for numerous custom upfits.
