Advanced Technology and Seamless Connectivity

Connecting driver and vehicle, the 2025 Base F-150 Lightning offers a suite of electronic systems designed to enhance convenience and command.

Infotainment and Interface

A 12-inch digital instrument cluster coupled with a 12-inch touchscreen featuring Ford Sync 4 software provides intuitive access to vehicle settings, navigation, and media. Both Apple CarPlay and Android Auto come standard with wireless support, simplifying smartphone integration for drivers navigating the greater Seattle area.

Additionally, wireless charging pads and a 360-degree camera system offer added convenience and safety, assisting in tight parking situations along Downtown Kent.

Driving Enhancements and Hands-Free Capability

The Base trim is equipped with Ford's advanced driver-assist technology suite, including the upgraded BlueCruise version 1.4. This hands-free driving system operates on nearly 97% of mapped U.S. and Canadian highways, allowing for more relaxed highway drives on I-5 or SR-512.

Additional features include lane change assist and in-lane repositioning, both aimed at reducing driver fatigue during long commutes or highway cruising across the Puget Sound region.
