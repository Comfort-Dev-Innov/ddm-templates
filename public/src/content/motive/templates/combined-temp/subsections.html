Family-First Innovations: Introducing "Baby Mode"

Hyundai's commitment to thoughtful design is perfectly encapsulated in a new, exclusive feature for the 2025 Tucson Hybrid and PHEV models: "Baby Mode". This innovation showcases an empathetic approach to engineering, designed to solve a real-world problem for families.

What is Baby Mode?

It is a driver-selectable setting, easily accessed through the "My Drive" menu, that is specifically calibrated to create the smoothest, most peaceful ride possible.

How Does it Work?

When activated, Baby Mode adjusts the powertrain's behavior by softening the initial throttle response. It smooths out acceleration and braking inputs to eliminate the sudden, jarring movements that can often startle a sleeping infant or an anxious pet.

The applications for this feature extend beyond just parents of young children. For passengers who are prone to motion sickness, the gentler ride dynamics can make a significant difference in comfort. It's for any driver who prioritizes a serene and seamless journey. This feature, combined with the available "Sounds of Nature" ambient soundscapes that can play calming audio like "Rainy Day" or "Lively Forest" through the speakers, demonstrates a deep understanding of passenger well-being.
