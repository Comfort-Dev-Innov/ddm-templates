Towing Capacity

The 2025 Santa Fe is built to haul. For models equipped with the 2.5T engine, you get a robust standard towing capacity of 3,500 pounds, which is more than enough for many small trailers and weekend toys.

However, for the serious adventurer, the XRT trim stands apart. It elevates the Santa Fe's capability with an increased towing capacity of up to 4,500 pounds. This significant upgrade opens up a world of possibilities for Oklahoma families. It's enough to confidently tow a fishing boat for a day on the water, or to haul a trailer loaded with ATVs for an exciting weekend.

The efficient Hybrid models are also impressively capable, offering a towing capacity of 2,000 pounds—perfect for personal watercraft, pop-up campers, or small utility trailers. To make every towing experience safer and more convenient, all 2025 Santa Fe models come standard with Trailer Sway Control and a trailer pre-wire connection, so you're ready to go from day one.

If your weeks are filled with highway travel and your weekends are dedicated to adventure—towing the boat, heading to the deer lease, or taking a road trip to the Ozarks—the powerful and capable 2.5T engine is your ideal partner. Conversely, if your primary focus is navigating the daily commute with maximum efficiency, enjoying a quiet and smooth ride in city traffic, and reducing your fuel budget, the smart and sophisticated 1.6T Hybrid is tailor-made for you.

By considering what your typical week looks like, you can easily identify the powertrain that will best serve your needs.
