HTRAC® All-Wheel Drive: Confidence in All Four Oklahoma Seasons

Oklahoma's weather is famously unpredictable. While we are blessed with a lot of sunshine, we also know that conditions can change in an instant, bringing sudden downpours, slick roads, and even the occasional dusting of ice or snow.

This is where Hyundai's available HTRAC® All-Wheel Drive system provides invaluable peace of mind.

HTRAC® is an intelligent, active on-demand AWD system that constantly monitors driving conditions. It can variably distribute power between the front and rear wheels to ensure you have maximum traction and stability precisely when you need it. Whether you're navigating a rain-soaked Kilpatrick Turnpike, encountering a patch of black ice on a frosty morning in your Yukon neighborhood, or simply want more confident handling on a gravel road, HTRAC® enhances your control.

The system works in conjunction with the Drive Mode Select feature, which includes a dedicated "Snow" mode that optimizes power delivery for slippery conditions, ensuring you can get where you need to go safely, no matter what the Oklahoma sky has in store.
