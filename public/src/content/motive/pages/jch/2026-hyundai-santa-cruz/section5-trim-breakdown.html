The Definitive Trim-by-Trim Breakdown

Choosing the right vehicle often comes down to finding the perfect combination of features and value. Here's a detailed look at what each trim level of the Santa Cruz and Frontier offers.


2026 Hyundai Santa Cruz Trim Walk

SE: The smart starting point.

The SE trim is impressively equipped, featuring standard 18-inch alloy wheels, Bi-LED projector headlights, wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, and a robust suite of Hyundai SmartSense safety features, including Blind-Spot Collision Warning and Rear Cross-Traffic Collision-Avoidance Assist. It's the perfect choice for the value-conscious buyer who wants modern SUV features with the added utility of an open bed.

SEL: The popular upgrade.

The SEL builds on the SE by adding key convenience features like an 8-way power driver seat with lumbar support, heated front seats, and Proximity Key with push-button start. This trim represents a sweet spot of comfort and technology for the average consumer.

SEL Activity: The lifestyle package.

This trim is a fantastic value, bundling desirable features that enhance the Santa Cruz's adventure-ready nature. It adds a power sunroof, the highly practical integrated tonneau cover, roof side rails, and wireless device charging. It's aimed at the active individual who will frequently use the bed for gear and wants a more premium, open-air experience.

XRT: The rugged adventurer.

The XRT marks a significant step up in performance and style. It makes the powerful 281-hp turbocharged engine and HTRAC AWD standard. It also gains a more aggressive look with unique 18-inch XRT alloy wheels, wider fender flares, and a dark-finish XRT grille and exterior accents. This trim is for the buyer who demands maximum power and a vehicle that looks ready for any adventure.

Limited: The fully-loaded tech showcase.

The Limited is the pinnacle of the Santa Cruz lineup, transforming it into a premium vehicle that rivals luxury SUVs. It adds genuine leather seating surfaces, ventilated front seats, a Bose premium audio system, a large 12.3-inch touchscreen with navigation, and the full suite of advanced safety technology, including the Surround View Monitor and Blind-Spot View Monitor. It's designed for the consumer who wants a top-tier SUV experience with the unique utility of a pickup, with no compromises.

2026 Nissan Frontier Trim Walk

S: The essential workhorse.

Available in both the extended King Cab and four-door Crew Cab configurations, the S trim provides the fundamentals of a great truck. It comes standard with the powerful 3.8L V6 engine, an 8-inch touchscreen with Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, and the comprehensive Nissan Safety Shield® 360 suite of driver aids. It's an excellent choice for fleet use or for individuals needing a no-frills, powerful, and reliable work truck.

SV: The well-equipped middle ground.

The SV is the mainstream choice, upgrading to 17-inch alloy wheels, a power driver's seat, and premium cloth upholstery. For 2026, it can be customized with several new packages, including the stylish Dark Armor Package, which adds 17-inch black wheels, a blacked-out grille, and other dark accents for a stealthy look. An All-Weather Package is also available, adding heated seats and a heated steering wheel.

PRO-X: The desert-running specialist.

A unique offering in the truck market, the PRO-X provides the aggressive off-road appearance and hardware—including Bilstein® performance shocks and all-terrain tires—of the top-tier off-road model but in a lighter and more affordable Rear-Wheel Drive configuration. It's designed for the enthusiast who loves the rugged aesthetic and improved suspension performance but doesn't require 4WD capability.

PRO-4X: The ultimate off-roader.

This is the most capable Frontier, equipped with standard part-time 4WD, three steel skid plates, all-terrain tires, Bilstein® shocks, and the all-important electronic locking rear differential for maximum traction on difficult trails. For the ultimate enthusiast, the PRO-4X is the foundation for the new PRO-4X R by Roush, a factory-endorsed package that adds a 2-inch lift with a specialized Roush x Öhlins 2.0 Off-Road Suspension system, unique 17-inch titanium-finish wheels, and exclusive Roush styling, creating the most capable Frontier ever offered.
