The Ownership Advantage: Warranty, Maintenance, and Peace of Mind

Beyond the initial purchase, the long-term cost and convenience of ownership are critical factors in any vehicle decision. In this area, Hyundai provides one of the most compelling and comprehensive packages in the entire automotive industry.

Hyundai's "America's Best Warranty" is a cornerstone of its brand promise. The 2026 Santa Cruz is covered by a 5-year/60,000-mile new vehicle limited warranty and an industry-leading 10-year/100,000-mile powertrain limited warranty. This is a significant advantage over the 2026 Nissan Frontier, which comes with a 3-year/36,000-mile basic warranty and a 5-year/60,000-mile powertrain warranty. The difference in powertrain coverage, double the duration, and 40,000 more miles, provides an unparalleled level of long-term protection and peace of mind for Santa Cruz owners.

Furthermore, Hyundai includes Hyundai Complimentary Maintenance with every new vehicle. This program covers normal, factory-scheduled maintenance, including oil changes, oil filter changes, and tire rotations, for 3 years or 36,000 miles, whichever comes first. This program significantly reduces out-of-pocket ownership costs for the first three years. While some Nissan dealers may offer their own maintenance plans, a comprehensive, nationwide, complimentary program is not detailed by the manufacturer.

Both brands also provide roadside assistance. Hyundai's coverage lasts for 5 years with unlimited miles, offering help with common issues like dead batteries, flat tires, and lockouts. Nissan's roadside assistance is also comprehensive, with towing coverage typically lasting for 5 years or 60,000 miles.

When viewed as a whole, Hyundai's ownership package presents a powerful value proposition. The combination of a longer warranty and included scheduled maintenance provides a tangible financial benefit and instills confidence, making a strong case for the Santa Cruz as the smarter long-term investment.
