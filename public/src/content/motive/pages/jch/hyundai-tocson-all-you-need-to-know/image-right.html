What's the Best Value Hyundai Tucson Trim Level?

For most buyers, the value sweet spot in the 2025 Tucson lineup is:

  • the SEL trim with the optional Convenience Package (on the gas model) or

  • the SEL Convenience trim (on the Hybrid model)

This combination unlocks the most sought-after technology and comfort upgrades without the full price jump to the Limited trim. It provides the stunning 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, premium H-Tex seating surfaces, a power sunroof, and the advanced Highway Driving Assist (HDA) system.
