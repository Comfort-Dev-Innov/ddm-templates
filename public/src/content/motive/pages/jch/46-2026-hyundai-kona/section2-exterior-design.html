The 2026 Kona and 2026 Niro Exterior Appeal and Design Flair

2026 Hyundai Kona

The Hyundai Kona showcases a bolder, contemporary exterior that turns heads along the streets in the city. Its sculpted body panels, active grille shutters, and available 19-inch N Line alloy wheels give it a dynamic stance that feels youthful yet refined.

Distinctive LED projector headlights and daytime running lights are standard, perfectly framing the Kona's assertive face. Select trims offer gloss black mirror caps with integrated LED signals, an upscale touch that adds a sense of premium craftsmanship.

  • Sculpted body panels with active grille shutters
  • Available 19-inch N Line alloy wheels
  • LED projector headlights & daytime running lights
  • Gloss black mirror caps with integrated LED signals
  • Dynamic, youthful stance with premium craftsmanship

2026 Kia Niro

Meanwhile, the Kia Niro's styling aligns more closely with a traditional crossover, representing its focus on eco-friendly credentials. While clean and functional, the Niro does not provide the same flair or road presence as the Kona and lacks the offering of larger, sportier wheel options seen in the Kona's SEL Premium and Limited trims.

  • Traditional crossover styling
  • Eco-friendly design credentials
  • Clean and functional appearance
  • Limited sportier wheel options
  • Conservative road presence
