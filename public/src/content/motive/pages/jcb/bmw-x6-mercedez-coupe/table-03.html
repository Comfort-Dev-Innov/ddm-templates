The cabin of the 2026 BMW X6 balances exquisite craftsmanship with cutting-edge tech integration, creating an environment both inviting and performance-oriented for families in Bethany and professionals commuting through Piedmont.

  • Seating and Space: Upholstered in Sensafin standard seating, with optional extended Merino leather, the X6 comfortably seats five with generous front legroom (40.4") and rear space (35.7"). While the coupe-like silhouette slightly reduces rear headroom compared to the X5 sibling, seating comfort remains firmly in the premium segment.
  • Cargo Versatility: With 27.4 cubic feet of cargo space behind the second row, extending to nearly 60 cubic feet when folded, the X6 readily adapts to active Edmond household needs — from weekend getaways to daily errands.
  • Refined Atmosphere: Optional features include ambient configurable lighting, panoramic sky lounge roof embedded with LED, soft-close doors, and glass control switches — transforming the cabin into a sanctuary reflecting innovation and indulgence.
  • Infotainment Displays: The expansive curved BMW Display integrates a 14.9-inch touchscreen with a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, offering crystal-clear graphics and intuitive control via BMW's latest OS 8.5.
