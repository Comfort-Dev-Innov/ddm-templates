Mustang Speed and Performance

The Ford Mustang has an undeniable reputation as a fast car, and specific models prove this claim with impressive statistics. The 2013 Shelby GT500, for example, features a 5.8-liter supercharged V8 engine producing 662 horsepower, accelerating from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.5 seconds, with a top speed of 200 mph. For Oklahoma City thrill-seekers, these figures offer an exhilarating driving experience.

Newer models like the 2020 Shelby GT500 elevate performance further with 760 horsepower from a 5.2-liter supercharged V8 and a seven-speed dual-clutch transmission, reaching 0-60 mph in 3.3 seconds. Track-focused trims such as the GT350 and GT350R provide precise handling and aerodynamic enhancements suited for spirited driving.

Safety is a priority even at high speeds. Mustangs come equipped with advanced features like adaptive cruise control, blind-spot monitoring, and electronic stability control, ensuring that drivers in Yukon and surrounding communities can enjoy their Mustang with confidence.
