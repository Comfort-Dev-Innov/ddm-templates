Electric Truck Reimagined: Introduction to the 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning

The 2025 Ford F-150 Lightning Base introduces an innovative blend of traditional truck toughness and cutting-edge electric propulsion, bringing new energy to the sought-after Ford lineup. Rooted in the familiar body-on-frame architecture of the iconic F-150, this electric variant makes no compromises on capability while delivering a cleaner, quieter ride suited for the varied roads of Kent and Tukwila.

This all-wheel-drive truck features dual electric motors for smooth power delivery and an independent rear suspension that enhances ride comfort and handling stability. Uniquely designed to serve both families and professionals, it strikes a balance between utility and modern technology, without the rugged, radical styling typical of many electric trucks.

Bowen Scarff Ford invites drivers from Covington and surrounding communities to explore this electric powerhouse that stands poised to redefine work and leisure driving. Combining Ford's legendary durability with next-generation electric power, the F-150 Lightning is built to handle everything from heavy equipment hauling to off-grid weekend adventures.
