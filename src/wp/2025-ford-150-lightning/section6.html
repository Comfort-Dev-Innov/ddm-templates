Comprehensive Safety and Driver Assist Systems

Safety is paramount with the 2025 F-150 Lightning Base, featuring an extensive array of protective technologies.

Ford Co-Pilot360 2.0 Suite

Standard across all trims, this system integrates multiple safety functions such as:

Lane-keeping assist to prevent unintentional drifting
Evasive steering assist that enhances maneuverability during emergency situations
Blind-spot monitoring with trailer coverage tailored for towing scenarios
Forward collision warning combined with automatic emergency braking
Hill descent control for controlled driving on steep gradients
Rear parking sensors to help with tight backing maneuvers
Adaptive cruise control optimizes speed and distance on highways
Automatic high-beam headlights for improved nighttime visibility

Safety Ratings and Protection

The F-150 Lightning proudly carries a five-star overall rating from NHTSA, affirming its leadership in occupant protection. Drivers in cities like Kirkland or Federal Way can expect enhanced confidence knowing their vehicle employs both passive and active safety systems designed for family and work scenarios.
