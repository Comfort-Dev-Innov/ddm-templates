Premium Interior Comfort and Cargo Innovation

Inside the cabin, the 2025 F-150 Lightning Base embraces functionality with a focus on durability and driver comfort, well-suited for long drives in cities such as SeaTac or Renton.

Spacious and Practical Cabin Layout

Designed for passenger comfort, the Base model features a spacious four-door layout that offers approximately 43.9 inches of front legroom and an impressive 43.6 inches of rear legroom. Materials are selected for long-lasting wear, with cloth seating providing breathable comfort while offering easy maintenance.

The redesigned center console maximizes storage without overcomplicating the space, while the absence of a traditional engine provides a cavernous front trunk offering around 400 pounds of additional cargo capacity. This "frunk" is ideal for storing smaller gear securely and protected from the weather, an advantage for outdoor enthusiasts near Snoqualmie.
