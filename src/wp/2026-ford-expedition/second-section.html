Ford Advantage

2026 Ford Expedition: A Modern Leader in Full-Size Utility

Refreshed engineering, new trims, and tech-rich comfort for Kent, WA families and adventurers.

The fifth-generation 2026 Ford Expedition arrives with refreshed engineering, new trims including the off-road Tremor, and a focus on tech-rich comfort. Targeting families and adventure seekers throughout Kent, WA, neighborhoods like Central to Renton, this SUV is designed as a versatile heavyweight offering an optimal balance of performance, luxury, and utility. Its broad trim lineup—from the practical Active model to the upscale King Ranch Max—caters to diverse tastes, all while maintaining Ford's reputation for durable construction and innovation.

Suburban Legacy

2026 Chevrolet Suburban: Tradition Meets Incremental Innovation

A spacious, familiar full-size SUV that leans on legacy and V8 strength.

The Chevrolet Suburban, now in its 12th generation, leverages a legacy of spaciousness and power, particularly appreciated in suburban Seattle communities such as Maple Valley and Covington. While its rear- and four-wheel drive choices and various trim levels (LS to High Country) offer customization, its core architecture stems from a 2025 redesign that prioritized preserving familiar ruggedness and interior volume. The Suburban’s V8 powertrains appeal to traditionalists but are increasingly challenged by more efficient, technologically advanced rivals.
