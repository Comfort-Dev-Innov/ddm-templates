Head-to-Head Overview and Recommendation

The 2025 Ford Explorer and Honda Pilot both cater to families seeking three-row SUVs with ample space and strong safety features. While both deliver reliable performance and competitive pricing near $40,000, the Explorer's 2025 refresh propels it ahead on several fronts.

  • The Explorer delivers a broader range of powertrain options featuring a potent twin-turbo V6 with up to 400 horsepower, substantially surpassing the Pilot's single naturally aspirated V6 output of 285 horsepower.
  • Its advanced Ford Digital Experience infotainment system with expansive touchscreen displays and integrated voice assistants marks a leap beyond Honda's more constrained 7- to 9-inch touchscreens.
  • With smart driver aids like BlueCruise hands-free highway driving, the Explorer offers cutting-edge convenience unavailable in the Pilot.
  • With its premium soft-touch materials and customizable lighting, the Explorer delivers a luxurious feel that goes far beyond the conventional design found in the Pilot.
  • Cargo versatility and towing capability also lean in favor of the Explorer, empowering drivers for family trips from Seattle to Tacoma with confidence.

This contrast positions the Ford Explorer as a compelling family-centered SUV choice in Kent and nearby communities. Bowen Scarff Ford's expert team encourages readers to experience the Explorer to appreciate its advanced engineering and comfort benefits fully.
