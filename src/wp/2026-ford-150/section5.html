Naturally Aspirated V8

5.0L Ti-VCT V8 Engine

400 Horsepower

The 5.0-liter naturally aspirated V8 engine remains a favorite among Ford enthusiasts who appreciate the classic V8 sound, linear power delivery, and robust capability. For 2026, this engine delivers an impressive 400 horsepower and 410 lb-ft of torque, making it well-suited for towing, payload, and daily driving.

This powertrain is ideal for those in the Kent and Renton areas who require a reliable workhorse that can handle towing trailers, boats, or heavy equipment. The 5.0L V8 supports a maximum payload capacity of up to 2,225 pounds and a towing capacity of approximately 12,900 pounds when properly equipped, which ranks it among the top in its class.
