Uncompromising Performance for Washington Roads

Under the hood, the 2026 Explorer offers two potent EcoBoost® engine options, both paired with a smooth 10-speed automatic transmission, ensuring you have the power you need whether merging onto I-5 or climbing a steep mountain pass.

  • Standard 2.3L EcoBoost® I-4: This robust engine is anything but basic. It delivers a commanding 300 horsepower and 310 lb.-ft. of torque, providing spirited acceleration and efficient performance for your daily drives around the Kent Valley.
  • Available 3.0L EcoBoost® V6: Standard on the ST and Platinum trims, this twin-turbocharged powerhouse unleashes an exhilarating 400 horsepower and 415 lb.-ft. of torque. This engine gives the Explorer the capability to tow up to 5,000 pounds when properly equipped—more than enough for a boat for a day on Lake Tapps or a small trailer for a camping trip in the Cascades.

Rear-wheel drive comes standard, offering a sporty feel, while available intelligent four-wheel drive provides enhanced traction and control, a must-have for slick roads and gravel paths leading to your favorite trailhead.

A Cabin of Comfort and Cutting-Edge Tech

Step inside the 2026 Explorer and you'll find a completely redesigned cabin focused on comfort, connectivity, and a premium experience. The dashboard has been pushed forward to create a more open and airy feel, and it's adorned with higher-quality materials and soft-touch surfaces throughout.

The star of the new interior is the Ford Digital Experience infotainment system. This new platform seamlessly integrates your digital life into the vehicle.

Key Technology Features:

  • Standard 13.2-inch Touchscreen: This vibrant, landscape-oriented screen is your command center for navigation, entertainment, and vehicle settings.
  • Standard 12.3-inch Digital Instrument Cluster: Customize the information you see, from your speed and fuel economy to detailed navigation prompts.
  • Google Built-in: With Google Maps as the native navigation system, you get real-time traffic updates and easy-to-use routing. Google Assistant enables you to control various functions with simple voice commands, such as "Hey Google, find the nearest coffee shop."
  • The Google Play Store: Download your favorite apps, such as Spotify and Audible, directly to the vehicle, so your entertainment is always ready to go, even without your phone.
