Power and Capability for Every Washington Adventure

Under the hood, the 2025 Expedition continues to rely on the proven and powerful 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6 engine. This isn't just any V6; its twin-turbocharged design delivers the V8-like power you need for merging onto I-5 with confidence, while offering greater efficiency.

The engine is offered in two variants:

Engine Configuration Horsepower (est.) Torque (est.) Key Benefit
Standard 3.6L EcoBoost V6 400 hp 480 lb.-ft. Excellent balance of power and efficiency for daily driving.
High-Output 3.5L EcoBoost V6 440 hp 510 lb.-ft. Maximum performance for towing and demanding tasks.

Transmission & Towing Power

Paired with a smooth 10-speed SelectShift automatic transmission, the powertrain provides responsive acceleration and intelligent gear changes. For those who plan to tow a boat to Lake Tapps or a travel trailer into the Cascades, the Expedition's capability is second to none. When equipped with the Heavy-Duty Trailer Tow Package, it boasts a maximum towing capacity of up to 9,300 pounds.
