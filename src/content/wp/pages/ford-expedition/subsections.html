A Redesigned Interior: Your Command Center of Comfort

The 2025 model year features a significantly refreshed interior, with a focus on technology and premium materials. The centerpiece is the stunning, vertically oriented touchscreen display, similar to the one found in the F-150 Lightning and Mustang Mach-E. This screen will run Ford's latest SYNC infotainment system, offering wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, intuitive navigation, and over-the-air software update capabilities.

  • Spacious Seating for Eight: The standard Expedition offers generous room across three rows. Safety on Kent Roads and Beyond

Ford prioritizes safety, and the 2025 Expedition comes standard with the Ford Co-Pilot360 suite of driver-assist technologies. This integrated system acts as an extra set of eyes on the road, providing peace of mind for you and your family.

Standard and available features include:

  • Pre-Collision Assist with Automatic Emergency Braking: Detects potential collisions with vehicles or pedestrians and automatically applies the brakes if necessary.
  • BLIS (Blind Spot Information System) with Cross-Traffic Alert: Alerts you to vehicles in your blind spot and can also detect traffic behind you when you're backing out of a parking spot.
  • Lane-Keeping System: Helps you stay centered in your lane.
  • Pro Trailer Backup Assist 2.0: Makes backing up a trailer as easy as turning a knob, a must-have for seasoned and novice towers alike.

Trim Levels to Match Your Lifestyle

The 2025 Expedition will be available in a range of trim levels, each offering a unique blend of features and style. From the rugged and off-road-ready Timberline to the ultimate luxury of the Platinum, there is an Expedition for every Kent family.

  • XL/STX: The smart, value-packed starting point.
  • XLT: The popular choice, adding more comfort and convenience features.
  • Timberline: For the adventurous, featuring all-terrain tires, higher ground clearance, and unique off-road tuning.
  • Limited: Introduces a host of luxury features and sophisticated styling.
  • King Ranch: Offers a signature Western-themed luxury experience with premium leather and exclusive details.
  • Platinum: The pinnacle of the lineup, boasting the high-output engine, premium amenities, and the most advanced technology.
