Discontinued Packages for 2025: What's No Longer Available?

For those interested in package options, the 2025 Ford F-150 marked the end of the Heritage Edition package, which debuted in 2023 as a nostalgic nod to Ford's past with distinctive two-tone paint schemes inspired by classic Blue Oval pickups of the 1970s through 1990s. This package was designed to celebrate Ford's 120th anniversary and 75 years of the F-Series legacy.

Heritage Edition Package Details

The Heritage Edition was available exclusively on XLT High (303A) models with a 145-inch wheelbase and the twin-turbo 3.5L V6 EcoBoost engine. It included unique exterior paint themes, black running boards, a honeycomb grille, 20-inch gloss black wheels, and a special interior with Heritage logos and slate gray accents.

Exclusive Features Included:
Unique exterior paint themes inspired by classic Ford pickups
Black running boards for enhanced style and functionality
Distinctive honeycomb grille design
20-inch gloss black wheels
Special interior with Heritage Edition logos
Slate gray interior accents

With the Heritage Edition package no longer offered for 2025 and absent from the 2026 lineup, buyers seeking retro styling cues might consider custom aftermarket accessories or select trims with appearance packages available at Bowen Scarff Ford.

Additional Change for 2025

Another notable change is the removal of the SecuriCode keypad from the standard features of XLT trims and above, which is now available only as a dealer-installed option. This adjustment reflects Ford's evolving approach to security and convenience features.
