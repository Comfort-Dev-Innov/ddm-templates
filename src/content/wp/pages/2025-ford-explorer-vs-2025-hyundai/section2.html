Ford Innovation

2025 Ford Explorer

Modern Tradition Meets Innovation

The sixth-generation Explorer, refined notably for 2025, balances rugged capability with premium refinement. It offers seating for six or seven, depending on trim, across four distinct versions from the capable Active to the performance-oriented ST model. Rear-wheel drive is standard, with intelligent all-wheel-drive options enhancing traction.

Key to its appeal are the sophisticated powertrains: a highly efficient 2.3-liter turbocharged four-cylinder producing a robust 300 hp, and an aggressive 3.0-liter twin-turbo V6 pushing 400 hp for thrill-seekers. This SUV thrives on suburban commutes, adventurous getaways near Snoqualmie, and family cargo hauling with ease.

Honda Reliability

2025 Honda Pilot

Dependable Practicality

The Pilot, in its fourth generation since 2023, continues as a reliable family SUV with straightforward utility. Up to eight occupants can be accommodated, and the naturally aspirated 3.5-liter V6 delivers a modest 285 hp. Front-wheel drive is standard, with all-wheel drive available on upper trims like the TrailSport and Black Edition.

The Pilot emphasizes usability, with flexible seating and practical cargo space. While it offers solid safety accommodations and off-road touches on TrailSport, it primarily targets buyers prioritizing function and refinement over performance edge.
