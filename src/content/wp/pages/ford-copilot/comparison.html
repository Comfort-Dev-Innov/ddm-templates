Co-Pilot360 vs. Co-Pilot360 Assist+

Ford offers its Co-Pilot360 technology in different packages, and it's helpful to understand what sets the Assist+ suite apart. The Ford Co-Pilot360 package comes standard on many new Ford vehicles. It features an impressive array of safety technologies, including a Blind Spot Information System with Cross-Traffic Alert, a Lane-Keeping System, Pre-Collision Assist with Automatic Emergency Braking, and Auto High-Beam Headlamps.

The Ford Co-Pilot360 Assist+ package includes all of those standard features and then adds the more advanced technologies detailed in this article. Essentially, the "plus" signifies the addition of Intelligent Adaptive Cruise Control with Stop-and-Go and Lane Centering, Evasive Steering Assist, and the Voice-Activated Touchscreen Navigation System. Choosing the Assist+ package elevates the driving experience from one of foundational safety to one of enhanced comfort and convenience, particularly for those who spend a significant amount of time in traffic or on long highway journeys.
