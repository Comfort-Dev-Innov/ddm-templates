In‑Cabin Technology & Connectivity

Technology and Connectivity Superiority

Expedition Focus

Integrated Assistants & Always‑On Connectivity

The 2026 Expedition integrates Google Assistant and Amazon Alexa voice controls that handle a range of functions, from calls and texts to smart home management—capabilities not as seamlessly embedded in the Suburban’s infotainment system. Ford's Connectivity Package further enhances the experience with a Wi‑Fi hotspot supporting up to ten devices, Google Maps, streaming apps, and over‑the‑air software updates, keeping the vehicle current and versatile.

Suburban Contrast

Big Screen, Less Deep Integration

While the Suburban also flaunts a substantial 17.7‑inch infotainment touchscreen, with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto standard, it lacks the same depth of built‑in voice assistant support and advanced connectivity services available in the Expedition. Additionally, the Expedition’s 24‑inch digital gauge cluster offers superior customization and readability compared to the Suburban’s 11‑inch cluster.
