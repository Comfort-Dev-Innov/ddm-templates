Advanced Technology and Safety on the Go

The 2025 Ford Transit is more than just a cargo hauler; it's a mobile office equipped with the latest technology to keep you safe, connected, and efficient. The centerpiece of the modern cabin is the large 12-inch touchscreen running Ford's SYNC 4 infotainment system. With standard wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, plus available Enhanced Voice Recognition and Connected Navigation, staying on schedule and in touch has never been easier.

Ford also prioritizes safety with its standard Ford Co-Pilot360 technology, a suite of driver-assist features crucial for navigating dense traffic in the Kent area.

Standard and Available Safety Features:

  • Pre-Collision Assist with Automatic Emergency Braking
  • Lane-Keeping System
  • Reverse Brake Assist
  • Available Blind Spot Information System (BLIS) with Cross-Traffic Alert
  • Available Adaptive Cruise Control

These features act as an extra set of eyes on the road, helping to reduce driver fatigue and prevent accidents, protecting your most valuable assets: your employees and your vehicle.
