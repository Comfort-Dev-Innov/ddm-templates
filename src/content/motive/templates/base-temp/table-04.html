At-a-Glance Comparison: Used vs. Hyundai CPO

Feature Standard Used Car Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned (CPO)
Eligibility Any age, any mileage. Less than 6 model years old; under 80,000 miles.
Inspection Varies by seller; often a basic safety check or none at all. 173-point comprehensive quality assurance inspection by a Hyundai-trained technician.
Warranty Typically sold "as-is" or with a limited, short-term dealer warranty. Reinstatement of the 10-Year/100,000-Mile Powertrain Limited Warranty plus the remainder of the 5-Year/60,000-Mile New Vehicle Limited Warranty.
Reconditioning May have cosmetic or mechanical issues. Repairs are not guaranteed to use manufacturer parts. Any necessary repairs or replacements are made with Genuine Hyundai Parts to meet manufacturer standards.
Vehicle History Buyer is responsible for obtaining and interpreting a history report. A complimentary, clean-title CARFAX® Vehicle History Report is required and provided.
Roadside Assistance Not included unless purchased separately. 10 years of unlimited mileage Roadside Assistance is included.
Special Benefits None. Rental car and travel interruption reimbursement for covered repairs; 3-month trial of SiriusXM® All-Access satellite radio.
Financing Standard used car financing rates apply, which are often higher. Often eligible for special financing rates from Hyundai Financial Services, similar to new car rates.
