In the world of high-performance off-road trucks, the Ford Raptor nameplate reigns supreme.

For 2025, Ford offers a formidable trio of trail-conquering machines, each engineered with a distinct purpose and personality. From the legendary full-size F-150 Raptor and its awe-inspiring V8-powered sibling, the F-150 Raptor R, to the agile and pugnacious Ford Ranger Raptor, there is a predator for every type of terrain.
