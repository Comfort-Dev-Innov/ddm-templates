Engineered Aggression: Exterior Design and Presence

A Raptor is identifiable from a mile away. All three trucks share the signature FORD grille, muscular fender flares, and an aggressive, wide stance.

The Ranger Raptor packs this attitude into athletic proportions. Its design is lean and purposeful, with every vent and crease optimized for performance.

It looks coiled and ready to strike, perfectly reflecting its agile nature.

The F-150 Raptor and Raptor R project an aura of sheer dominance. Their immense width and height, combined with massive tires (up to 37 inches), give them an undeniable and intimidating presence.

The Raptor R distinguishes itself further with unique Code Orange accents, a power dome on the hood, and graphics that celebrate its supercharged V8 heart.
