While every Certified Pre-Owned vehicle is technically "used," a vast gulf separates a Certified Pre-Owned Hyundai from a typical used car. Understanding this distinction is the first step toward making the right decision for your needs.

What is a standard Used Car?

A "used car" is a term for any vehicle that has had at least one previous owner. These vehicles represent a wide spectrum of age, mileage, condition, and history. You can find them through private sellers, independent used car lots, or as part of a dealership's non-certified inventory.

The primary appeal of a standard used car is its lower upfront cost.

However, this affordability often comes with a higher degree of uncertainty. Most used cars are sold "as-is," which means you accept the vehicle in its current state, and the seller has no obligation to cover repairs after the sale. While we thoroughly inspect all used vehicles on our lot to ensure they are safe and operational, they don't undergo the rigorous, manufacturer-mandated process required for certification. The responsibility for verifying the vehicle's mechanical health and history largely falls on you, the buyer.

What is a Hyundai Certified Pre-Owned Vehicle?

To even be considered for the Hyundai CPO program, a vehicle must meet strict initial criteria: it must be no more than six model years old and have fewer than 80,000 miles on the odometer.

If it meets these requirements, it then undergoes a comprehensive 173-point quality assurance inspection conducted by a factory-trained Hyundai technician. This exhaustive process covers everything from the engine and transmission performance to the function of the navigation system and the condition of the paint. Any component that doesn't meet Hyundai's high standards is repaired or replaced with a Genuine Hyundai Part.

This meticulous process ensures that a CPO Hyundai offers a "like-new" experience with the value of a pre-owned price tag. It's a vehicle that provides proven quality and, most importantly, unparalleled peace of mind backed directly by the manufacturer.
