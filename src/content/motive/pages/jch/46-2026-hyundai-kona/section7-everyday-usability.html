Everyday Usability: Cargo Capacity

The Kona excels in functional versatility. Dual-row USB ports ensure all passengers stay connected, while wireless charging is curtailed to higher trims, enhancing convenience for tech-reliant drivers around Proviso Township.

Cargo capacity favors the Kona with more generous rear volume and folding seat flexibility. Its higher payload and towing capacity (though modest) give it an edge over the Niro, which suffers from rear cargo limitations due to hybrid battery placement.

Practical features such as automatic high beams, LED lighting, and a hands-free smart liftgate in top trims underscore Kona's thoughtful design focus that improves daily driving ease.
