A Cabin for Every Crew: Versatility and Space

The Santa Fe's expanded dimensions and thoughtful layout create a remarkably spacious and flexible interior. Most trims come standard with a seven-seat configuration, featuring a second-row bench seat that is perfect for maximizing passenger capacity.

If you prioritize second-row comfort, the top-tier Calligraphy trim offers a luxurious six-seat layout with plush captain's chairs that provide generous personal space and a convenient pass-through to the third row.

The interior dimensions speak for themselves, with generous head, shoulder, and legroom in all three rows to ensure that passengers of all sizes can travel in comfort.

There is plenty of room for growing teenagers in the second row, and enough space for everyone to feel relaxed and comfortable, even on a longer road trip to a destination like Arkansas, or Dallas, Texas.

When it's time to haul gear instead of people, the Santa Fe transforms into a cargo king.

With all seats in place, you still have a useful 14.6 cubic feet of space behind the third row.

Fold the third row down, and that expands to a cavernous 40.5 cubic feet.

For maximum capacity, folding both the second and third rows flat opens up a massive 79.6 cubic feet of space.

This is more than enough room for a full load of luggage, sports equipment for a weekend tournament, or a big shopping haul.
