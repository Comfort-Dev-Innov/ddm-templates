Hyundai has equipped the 2025 Santa Fe with an extensive suite of SmartSense safety and driver-assistance technologies designed to act as your vigilant co-pilot. These systems use a network of cameras and sensors to monitor your surroundings, providing alerts and even intervening to help you avoid potential hazards. This commitment to safety provides invaluable peace of mind for you and your family on every Oklahoma road.

An Extra Set of Eyes on the Highway

Long drives on Oklahoma's turnpikes and interstates can be fatiguing. The Santa Fe's available Highway Driving Assist (HDA) systems are designed to reduce that strain. HDA, standard on the XRT and Limited trims, uses adaptive cruise control and lane-keeping functions to help you maintain a set speed and distance from the vehicle ahead, while also keeping you centered in your lane. This makes a long trip down the H.E. Bailey Turnpike or I-44 to Tulsa feel significantly more relaxed.

The Calligraphy trim upgrades to Highway Driving Assist 2 (HDA2), which adds the ability to assist with lane changes when you activate the turn signal. Furthermore, on trims with built-in navigation, the Smart Cruise Control system is enhanced with Curve Control, which can anticipate an upcoming curve in the highway and proactively adjust your speed for a smoother, safer transit.

Confidence for Navigating the City

Navigating busy urban environments is made easier and safer with the Santa Fe's city-focused safety features. Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist (FCA) is standard on all trims and is designed to detect potential collisions with vehicles, pedestrians, or cyclists ahead. It can provide a warning and automatically apply the brakes if necessary, a crucial feature for unpredictable traffic.

Parking and maneuvering in tight spaces are simplified with the available Surround View Monitor (SVM) and Blind-Spot View Monitor (BVM). The SVM provides a 360-degree, top-down view of your surroundings, while the BVM displays a live video feed of your blind spot directly in the digital instrument cluster. These systems give you unparalleled awareness, taking the stress out of navigating a crowded parking garage or changing lanes on I-235 during peak traffic.

For the ultimate in parking convenience, the available Remote Smart Parking Assist (RSPA) allows you to use your smart key to guide the Santa Fe into or out of a tight parking space while you stand safely outside the vehicle.
