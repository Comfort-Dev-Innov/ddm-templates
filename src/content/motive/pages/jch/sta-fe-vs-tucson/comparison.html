Ideal Buyer: Who Should Choose the Santa Fe — and Who Should Go for the Tucson?

After breaking down all the details, the choice comes down to you and your lifestyle. Let's create some profiles of Oklahoma drivers to see who fits best behind the wheel of each SUV.

Team Tucson

Team Tucson

The Tucson is for those who need flexibility, style, and efficiency.

Long daily commutes:
The Tucson Hybrid Blue delivers an incredible 38 MPG combined, saving significant money at the pump, while standard HTRAC AWD provides confidence during unexpected spring storms.
Urban driving and tight parking:
The Tucson Limited's sleek design stands out in the city, with Remote Smart Parking Assist for tight spots. Ventilated seats are essential for hot summer days.
Weekend adventures with weekday practicality:
The Tucson with HTRAC AWD offers nimble city driving during the week, then fold the seats down for massive cargo space—perfect for loading camping gear for state park trips.
Team Santa Fe

Team Santa Fe

The Santa Fe is the command center for busy families.

School runs and carpooling:
The Santa Fe SEL's standard seven-passenger seating handles daily school runs and sports practice carpools. The hands-free smart liftgate is invaluable when juggling sports bags and groceries.
Tournament travel and sports families:
The Santa Fe XRT's rugged looks, all-terrain tires, and standard HTRAC AWD provide confidence on any road, in any weather. Ample cargo space accommodates coolers, chairs, and team equipment.
Long drives with comfort priorities:
The Santa Fe Calligraphy's powerful 2.5T engine handles everything with ease, while premium Nappa leather interior and second-row captain's chairs make every trip feel first-class.
