Bluelink+ for Parental Peace of Mind

For parents, the Hyundai advantage extends even when they aren't in the car. The Bluelink+ Connected Car System, available on many models, is the ultimate tool for staying connected and helping guide a teen's driving habits. Through a simple smartphone app, parents can set up:

  • Geo-Fencing:
    Create virtual boundaries around areas like school or work and receive an alert if the vehicle enters or leaves them.
  • Speed Alerts:
    Set a maximum speed limit and get a notification if it's exceeded.
  • Curfew Alerts:
    Establish a time by which the car should be parked for the night.

These features are not about surveillance; they are about coaching. They provide parents with the information needed to have constructive conversations about responsible driving, directly addressing the key risk factors like speeding and late-night driving that are statistically linked to teen accidents.

It's peace of mind, delivered through technology.
