The Exterior and Interior Cabin Design: Santa Cruz over Frontier

The interior design of each vehicle is a direct reflection of its overall philosophy, with the Santa Cruz offering a tech-forward, SUV-like sanctuary and the Frontier providing a functional, durable command center.

The cabin of the 2026 Santa Cruz is a showcase of modern design, closely mirroring the sophisticated interior of the Tucson SUV. It features a driver-centric, wraparound cockpit that creates an immersive experience. On upper trims like the XRT and Limited, this is elevated by a stunning "glass cockpit" effect, created by a seamless panel housing both a 12.3-inch fully digital instrument cluster and a 12.3-inch high-resolution touchscreen navigation system. The widespread availability of wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™, along with premium features like a Bose audio system and the innovative Hyundai Digital Key, which allows your smartphone to function as a key, cements its position as the technology leader.

The 2026 Frontier's interior is designed with ruggedness and straightforward functionality in mind. The layout is more traditional and upright, with large, easy-to-use controls that can be operated even with gloves on. The materials are chosen for durability, fitting the vehicle's work-ready persona. While it is equipped with modern technology, including a standard 8-inch touchscreen on the S trim and a large 12.3-inch screen on SV and PRO grades, the overall design is more conservative. Wireless smartphone integration is available on SV and higher trims, and a premium Fender audio system is optional. The Frontier's cabin is a comfortable and well-built space that feels perfectly suited to a traditional truck.

For the buyer cross-shopping with compact SUVs, the Santa Cruz's interior will feel both familiar and aspirational, offering a clear advantage in perceived quality and technological "wow factor." For the buyer who is already a truck owner or prefers a more utilitarian aesthetic, the Frontier's durable and functional cabin will feel right at home.
