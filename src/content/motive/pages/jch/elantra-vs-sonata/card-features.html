A Shared Digital DNA

Both sedans offer a stunning, driver-focused digital cockpit. The Elantra is available with a beautiful dual-screen display, featuring a 10.25-inch fully digital instrument cluster and a 10.25-inch high-resolution touchscreen for navigation and infotainment. This setup creates a seamless, high-tech interface that is both visually impressive and intuitive to use.

The Sonata offers a similar concept, with a standard 12.3-inch central touchscreen and an available 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster. While the Sonata's screens are slightly larger, the core user experience and functionality are remarkably similar. Critically, essential features like wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration are available on both models, ensuring seamless connectivity for all users.

Premium Features

The Elantra offers a number of premium features that were once only found in luxury vehicles. Buyers can equip their Elantra with an 8-speaker Bose® premium sound system, a wireless device charging pad, durable and stylish H-Tex™ leatherette seating surfaces, heated front seats, and a power sunroof.

These are the same caliber of features available on the more expensive Sonata, which also offers a Bose® audio system, wireless charging, and heated seats. A fully equipped Elantra Limited provides a near-luxury cabin experience, complete with sophisticated materials and advanced technology, but at a price point that is significantly more accessible than a comparably equipped Sonata. This allows buyers to enjoy a premium experience without paying a premium price.

The Sonata's Exclusive Tech Features

To its credit, the Sonata does offer a few exclusive, high-end technology features. The most notable is the available Remote Smart Parking Assist (RSPA) on the Limited trim. This futuristic system allows the driver to remotely guide the Sonata into and out of tight parking spaces using the smart key. While an impressive piece of technology, it is a niche feature that underscores the Sonata's positioning as the more luxurious, tech-forward option for those who prioritize such cutting-edge conveniences.
