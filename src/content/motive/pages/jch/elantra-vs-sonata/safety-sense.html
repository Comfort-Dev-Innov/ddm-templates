Hyundai SmartSense™ for All

The foundation of this outstanding safety performance is the Hyundai SmartSense™ suite of advanced driver-assistance systems. This comprehensive safety net is available on both the Elantra and Sonata and includes crucial technologies designed to prevent accidents before they happen. Key features include:

  • Forward Collision-Avoidance Assist with Pedestrian Detection: Can warn the driver of an impending collision and automatically apply the brakes if necessary.
  • Lane Keeping Assist & Lane Following Assist: Helps keep the vehicle centered in its lane.
  • Blind-Spot Collision-Avoidance Assist: Can detect vehicles in the driver's blind spot and provide alerts or even braking assistance to prevent an unsafe lane change.
  • Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go: Maintains a set speed and distance from the vehicle ahead, capable of bringing the car to a complete stop and resuming in traffic.

The fact that both vehicles offer this identical, top-tier level of safety technology and have earned the same highest-possible safety awards from the IIHS dismantles the "bigger is safer" argument. The Elantra provides uncompromising safety at a more accessible price.
