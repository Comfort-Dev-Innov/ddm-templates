Choosing Your 2026 Hyundai Santa Cruz

The Santa Cruz trim walk progresses from a practical and well-equipped base model to a rugged and luxurious adventure vehicle. A key distinction in its lineup is the significant powertrain upgrade that occurs at the XRT level.

  • SE: The SE is the well-equipped workhorse of the lineup. It impresses with standard technology that includes a large 12.3-inch touchscreen and wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ integration.
  • SEL: This trim adds a layer of comfort and convenience with popular features like heated front seats, a power-adjustable driver's seat, and Proximity Key with push-button start.
  • SEL Activity: A pivotal trim for those focused on utility, the SEL Activity package adds a factory-integrated tonneau cover, LED bed lighting, a sunroof, and a wireless charging pad, making the cargo bed significantly more practical for everyday use.
  • XRT: The XRT marks a major turning point in the lineup, establishing a rugged, adventurous persona. It makes the powerful 281-hp turbocharged engine and HTRAC AWD standard. It also adds exclusive styling, all-terrain tires, and advanced driver aids like adaptive cruise control.
  • Limited: This is the fully-loaded alternative to a conventional truck. It combines the XRT's potent powertrain with premium luxury features, including leather upholstery, ventilated front seats, a Bose® premium audio system, and stylish 20-inch wheels.
