Choosing between the 2026 Hyundai IONIQ 9 and the 2026 Hyundai Santa Cruz comes down to lifestyle. The IONIQ 9 represents Hyundai’s electric future: a spacious, three-row SUV offering quiet performance, advanced technology, and long-range efficiency. The Santa Cruz, on the other hand, blends SUV comfort with truck-like utility, delivering a balance of refinement and everyday practicality.


Both vehicles showcase Hyundai’s innovation in different ways. The IONIQ 9 is ideal for drivers who value clean energy, family comfort, and cutting-edge design. The Santa Cruz is perfect for those who want flexibility; something that handles weekday commutes as easily as weekend projects or outdoor adventures.


In this guide, we’ll compare how these two stand apart in performance, technology, design, and ownership experience, helping you decide which one fits your needs best.
