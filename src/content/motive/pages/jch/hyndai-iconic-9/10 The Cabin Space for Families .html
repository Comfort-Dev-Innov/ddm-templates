The Cabin: Space for Families and Gear


The interior of a vehicle is where life happens, and the IONIQ 9 and Santa Cruz are designed around two different sets of priorities. The IONIQ 9 is conceived as a spacious, mobile lounge focused on passenger comfort, while the Santa Cruz acts as a durable command center for adventure, prioritizing practicality and clever storage.


The IONIQ 9 offers a cavernous cabin with a passenger volume of approximately 163.5 cubic feet, configurable for either six or seven occupants. Legroom is generous in all three rows, with up to 42.8 inches for the second row and a remarkable 32 inches for the third, making it comfortable for adults on longer journeys. Premium materials abound, from stain-resistant cloth on entry trims to luxurious H-TexÂ® surfaces, ambient lighting, and available first- and second-row Relaxation Seats with massage functions on upper trims. For a family from Norman heading to an OU football game, the IONIQ 9's seating capacity means you can bring friends along in comfort.


The Santa Cruz provides a comfortable and well-designed cabin for five passengers, with a total passenger volume of around 102-104 cubic feet. Rear legroom measures a respectable 36.5 inches, suitable for children and adults on most trips. The interior materials are chosen for durability and ease of maintenance, ranging from stain-resistant cloth to practical leatherette and premium leather on the Limited trim. For the couple picking up supplies from a local garden center, the Santa Cruz's durable interior is easy to clean and built to withstand an active lifestyle.
