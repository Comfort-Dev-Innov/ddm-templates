The 2025 Hyundai Kona N-Line

If you need the practicality of a compact SUV but refuse to blend in with the crowd, the 2025 Hyundai Kona N-Line offers the perfect solution. It wraps a turbocharged powertrain and an active-lifestyle-ready chassis in a bold, aggressive design. It's the ideal vehicle for hauling gear for a weekend adventure or navigating tight packing spaces.

Aggressive & Athletic Styling
