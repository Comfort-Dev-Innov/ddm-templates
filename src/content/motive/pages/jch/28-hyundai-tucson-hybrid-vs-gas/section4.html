Inside the 2025 Tucson

One of the most dramatic updates for the 2025 Tucson is its completely redesigned interior, which creates a driver-focused environment that is both technologically advanced and beautifully intuitive.

The Panoramic Curved Display

The centerpiece of the new cabin is the available panoramic curved display. This stunning feature seamlessly integrates a 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster and a 12.3-inch infotainment touchscreen into a single, elegant unit that sweeps across the dashboard. This is a design statement that gives the Tucson a sophisticated, futuristic feel. The system itself is faster, with enhanced graphics and quicker processing speeds, making every interaction smooth and responsive.

Effortless Connectivity

Hyundai understands that seamless connection to your digital life is essential. That's why every 2025 Tucson, from the SE on up, comes standard with wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™. This means you can access your favorite maps, music, and messaging apps on the large touchscreen without ever needing to plug in your phone. In a thoughtful nod to user-friendliness, Hyundai has also brought back physical knobs for volume and tuning, along with dedicated switchgear for the climate controls, making common adjustments quick, easy, and safe to perform while driving.

Your Smartphone is the Key: Hyundai Digital Key 2

The available Hyundai Digital Key 2 technology transforms how you interact with your vehicle. This feature allows you to use your compatible iPhone, Apple Watch, or Samsung/Google smartphone to securely lock, unlock, and start your Tucson. The system uses two forms of technology. For many devices, you can simply use Near Field Communication (NFC) by tapping your phone on the driver's door handle to unlock it. For newer, UWB-equipped devices and vehicles, the experience is even more magical: the car can sense your approach and unlock the doors for you automatically, hands-free.

Perhaps the most powerful aspect of Digital Key 2 is the ability to securely share a key with up to three other people through your phone's wallet app. This is a perfect solution for Oklahoma families. You can grant a teenager access to the car for a specific period without handing over a physical key, or send a key to a visiting family member from across the country. It's the ultimate in modern convenience and control.
