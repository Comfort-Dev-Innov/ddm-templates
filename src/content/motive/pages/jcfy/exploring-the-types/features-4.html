The 2025 Ford Lineup: What's New at Joe Cooper Ford of Yukon?

The 2025 Ford model year brings exciting updates and new options that enhance performance, comfort, and sustainability.

Trucks and Pickups

F-150 and F-150 Lightning

The F-150 remains a top choice with traditional and hybrid models. The Lightning introduces a powerful all-electric option with fast charging and impressive range.

Super Duty Series

Updated with advanced towing technologies and luxurious interiors, ideal for commercial and heavy-duty needs.

Maverick and Ranger

The Maverick offers a compact hybrid pickup experience, while the Ranger delivers a solid mid-size truck option.

SUVs and Crossovers

Escape, Explorer, Bronco & Expedition

The 2025 lineup continues to feature the Escape, Explorer, Bronco, and Expedition with updated infotainment systems, enhanced driver assistance, and hybrid variants.

Mustang Mach-E

The Mustang Mach-E leads Ford's electric SUV charge with extended range and performance upgrades.

Commercial Vehicles

Transit and E-Transit

The Transit and E-Transit continue to support businesses with customizable configurations and electric powertrain options, helping fleets in Yukon and Mustang reduce emissions and operating costs.
