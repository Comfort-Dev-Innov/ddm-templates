Understanding the Different Types of Ford Vehicles

Ford's vehicle lineup is remarkably diverse, engineered to meet the demands of various lifestyles and professional needs.

Drivers in communities like Union City and Piedmont can find options ranging from powerful work trucks to efficient family SUVs.

Trucks

The Backbone of Ford's Legacy

Ford's trucks, led by the iconic F-Series, are renowned for their durability and versatility. The F-150, available in traditional gasoline, hybrid, and all-electric (F-150 Lightning) versions, offers customizable cab sizes and bed lengths, making it a perfect companion for Oklahoma City's work sites or recreational towing.

The Super Duty lineup, encompassing the F-250, F-350, and beyond, caters to heavy-duty commercial needs, with towing capacities exceeding 40,000 pounds. The Ranger and Maverick add compact and mid-size truck options, blending fuel efficiency with Ford's signature ruggedness.

SUVs

Family-Friendly and Adventure-Ready

Ford's SUV range suits families in Edmond and Warr Acres, as well as outdoor enthusiasts. The compact Ford Escape offers excellent fuel economy and advanced tech features, ideal for daily commutes. For larger families or those needing more cargo space, the Explorer and Expedition provide three-row seating and powerful engines.

The Bronco and Bronco Sport emphasize off-road capability, appealing to drivers seeking adventure on Oklahoma's backroads and beyond.

Cars and Electric Vehicles

Cutting-Edge Technology

Though Ford's lineup has shifted toward SUVs and trucks, the Mustang remains a flagship muscle car, delivering aggressive styling and high performance. The Mustang Mach-E represents Ford's push into electric vehicles with competitive range and cutting-edge technology.

Other sedans like the Fusion and Taurus, while discontinued in North America, continue to have a presence in global markets including Ford's Indian lineup with models like the Figo and Aspire.

Commercial Vehicles

Cargo or Passenger Needs

Ford Pro, the commercial vehicle division, offers the Transit van series and E-Transit electric vans, supporting businesses from Yukon to Bethany with customizable configurations for cargo or passenger needs.
