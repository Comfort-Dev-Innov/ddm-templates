Transmission Choices and Shifting

Mustang buyers in Piedmont and surrounding areas have multiple transmission options to fit their driving style and preferences. The 2025 Ford Mustang models come with either a 10-speed SelectShift® automatic or a 6-speed manual transmission, delivering both convenience and engagement.

The 10-speed SelectShift® automatic transmission, standard on all EcoBoost trims and available on others, offers smooth gear changes and includes paddle shifters mounted on the steering wheel. This setup is ideal for drivers who want effortless gear shifts in traffic-heavy Oklahoma City commutes or relaxed cruising. The automatic's industry-first remote rev feature allows Mustang owners to trigger the engine's roar remotely, adding to the fun factor without sacrificing practicality.

For enthusiasts who enjoy complete control, the 6-speed manual transmission is standard on all GT trims and offers rev-matching technology. This ensures smooth gear changes at any speed, reducing wear and enhancing performance during spirited drives on open roads near Mustang or Edmond. The Dark Horse™ model offers an exclusive 6-speed manual transmission with no-lift shifting, allowing drivers to shift gears without lifting off the throttle, a feature prized by track-day enthusiasts.

Both transmission types meet modern performance standards and offer a driving experience tailored to various Oklahoma lifestyles. Whether navigating city streets or pushing the Mustang on country roads, you can choose a transmission that fits your needs.
