Performance That Commands the Road


Launching your journey in the 2026 BMW X6 means engaging with a suite of robust powertrain options offering a spectrum of power and agility tailored to your driving preferences and lifestyle demands across the broader Edmond area.


Base Powertrain: xDrive40i

The entry-level xDrive40i is equipped with a 3.0-liter turbocharged inline-six engine, paired with a 48-volt mild hybrid system, producing a potent 375 horsepower and 383 lb-ft of torque. This configuration accelerates the X6 with vigorous responsiveness, achieving 0-60 mph in approximately 5.2 seconds, according to BMW's estimates. Independent tests cite times closer to 4.6 seconds, referencing its sibling, the X5 xDrive40i. This powertrain offers a balanced combination of spirited performance and commendable fuel efficiency, rated at nearly 23 mpg city and 26 mpg highway, making it an ideal match for daily commuting and weekend excursions around Bethany or Mustang.


Enhanced M60i xDrive Trim

Stepping up the power curve, the M60i xDrive variant is equipped with a 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8 paired with mild hybrid assistance, delivering an exhilarating 523 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque. With an estimated sprint from zero to 60 mph in an impressive 4.2 seconds (likely closer to 3.8 seconds in real-world testing), the M60i combines luxury cruising with genuinely thrilling acceleration. Its adaptive M Suspension ensures dynamic handling without sacrificing control, supported by M Sport brakes for enhanced stopping power—perfect for spirited drives through the underserved roads of Guthrie or roads around Piedmont.


Performance Focus: M Competition

For enthusiasts prioritizing extreme sport performance, the M Competition model delivers a commanding 617 horsepower from its 4.4-liter twin-turbo V8, supported by a reinforced chassis, adaptive electronically controlled suspension, and performance exhaust tuning. Achieving 0-60 mph in just 3.5 seconds and topping out at 155 mph (extendable to 177 mph with the M Drivers Package), this version brings race-inspired agility and precision to the midsize luxury SUV segment. Features like the Active M rear differential translate this raw power into exceptional cornering grip and driving confidence, making it well-suited for navigating challenging routes in Choctaw or Bethany.
