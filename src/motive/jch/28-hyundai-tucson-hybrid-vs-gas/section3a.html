The 2025 Tucson Hybrid

More Power, More Efficiency, More Capability

The 2025 Tucson Hybrid lineup is designed for the driver who wants more: more power, more efficiency, and more standard capability. Every hybrid model comes standard with the formidable 231-horsepower turbocharged hybrid powertrain and the confidence-inspiring HTRAC® All-Wheel Drive system. The choice here isn't about whether to get the advanced powertrain, but rather which collection of features best complements its remarkable performance.

Blue Trim: Maximum Efficiency Champion

The mission of the Tucson Hybrid Blue is clear: to deliver the absolute maximum fuel efficiency possible without asking you to compromise on essential technology or comfort.

This trim is the ultimate weapon against fluctuating gas prices in Oklahoma City, achieving an incredible EPA-estimated 38 MPG combined (38 City/38 Highway). This is a remarkable feat for a spacious, all-wheel-drive SUV. Even as the efficiency leader, the Blue trim is generously equipped. You still get the large 12.3-inch touchscreen, Proximity Key with push-button start, heated front seats, and a full suite of Hyundai SmartSense safety features. It rides on stylish 17-inch alloy wheels that are optimized for aerodynamic efficiency, helping it achieve its stellar MPG figures.

SEL Convenience Trim: The Hybrid Sweet Spot

For many buyers, the Tucson Hybrid SEL Convenience will be the perfect sweet spot, masterfully blending impressive efficiency with a host of premium features.

It offers a very strong EPA-estimated 35 MPG combined, ensuring you still enjoy significant savings at the pump. Over the Blue trim, the SEL Convenience adds transformative upgrades that elevate the entire driving experience. The seating surfaces are upgraded to luxurious and durable H-Tex®, the driver is treated to the full 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, and the cabin is opened up to the sky with a massive panoramic sunroof. To set the mood, expanded 64-color ambient interior lighting is added to the center console and door pockets. This is the ideal choice for the driver who wants the long-term value of a hybrid without forgoing the touches of luxury that make every drive special.

N Line Trim: Sporty Performance Hybrid

There is a common question about Hyundai's N Line models: Are they faster? In the case of the Tucson Hybrid, the powertrain is the same potent 231-hp unit found in the other trims. The true purpose of the N Line is to provide the ultimate expression of sporty, aggressive design.

This is the Tucson for the driver who values a bold aesthetic and wants to turn heads, whether parked in Bricktown for dinner or arriving at a tailgate party for the big game. The N Line's transformation is comprehensive and striking. It features exclusive 19-inch N Line alloy wheels, a more aggressive front and rear fascia design, a unique single twin-tip exhaust, and numerous blacked-out exterior accents, including the side mirrors and headlight bezels. The interior is just as dramatic, with unique black sport seats that combine leather and cloth, vibrant red accent stitching, alloy sport pedals, and a leather-wrapped steering wheel bearing the N emblem. It's the perfect fusion of hybrid efficiency and motorsport-inspired style.

Limited Trim: The Ultimate Hybrid Experience

The Tucson Hybrid Limited combines the best of all worlds: the efficiency and power of the hybrid powertrain, the confidence of standard HTRAC® AWD, and the most advanced technology and luxury features Hyundai has to offer.

It shares the excellent 35 MPG combined rating of the SEL Convenience and N Line trims and builds upon their features to create a true flagship experience. The interior is adorned with full leather-trimmed seats, which are ventilated in the front and heated in both the front and rear. The Bose® Premium Audio system provides an immersive sound experience, while advanced tech like the Head-Up Display and Remote Smart Parking Assist adds a layer of futuristic convenience. For an extra measure of personalized security, the Limited features a fingerprint scanner that can be tied to your driver profile. The cabin is also exceptionally quiet, benefiting from acoustic-laminated glass on both the windshield and the front side windows, making it a peaceful sanctuary on the road.
