The Gas-Powered 2025 Tucson

A Trim for Everyone

Hyundai has masterfully crafted the gasoline-powered Tucson lineup to ensure there is a perfect model for every budget and lifestyle in the Oklahoma City area. Each trim builds upon the last, offering a clear and compelling value proposition.

SE Trim: The Smart and Feature-Rich Foundation

The SE should not be considered a "base" model, but rather the intelligent entry point into the Tucson family, delivering a remarkable level of technology and convenience that feels anything but standard.

It's the perfect choice for the value-conscious buyer who refuses to compromise on modern features. From the moment you approach the SE, the standard Proximity Key with push-button start offers a touch of premium convenience. Inside, you're greeted by the stunning, all-new 12.3-inch color touchscreen display, which serves as the vibrant hub for your infotainment and features standard wireless Apple CarPlay® and Android Auto™ compatibility. Your seats are covered in a durable, stain-resistant cloth, ready for the rigors of daily life, and you ride on stylish 17-inch alloy wheels. It's a complete package that offers exceptional function and affordability.

SEL Trim: The Perfect Balance of Comfort and Tech

The SEL trim is the ideal upgrade for the Oklahoma driver who desires more of the creature comforts that make the daily commute or a family road trip more enjoyable.

It takes the excellent foundation of the SE and adds a suite of sought-after features. The SEL introduces the revolutionary Hyundai Digital Key 2, which allows you to use your smartphone as your key. For those chilly winter mornings, you'll appreciate the heated front seats and the 8-way power-adjustable driver's seat with lumbar support that helps you find the perfect driving position. Convenience is also enhanced with a standard wireless device charging pad and a hands-free smart liftgate, which is a lifesaver when you're loading groceries or packing for a weekend away. For future adventures, standard roof side rails are ready for a rack and cargo carrier.

XRT Trim

If you embrace an active, outdoor lifestyle, the Tucson XRT is the perfect match. This trim is all about projecting a rugged, capable attitude, backed by features that enhance its adventurous spirit.

The XRT builds on the SEL's features and transforms the Tucson's appearance with exclusive, aggressive styling. You'll immediately notice the XRT-exclusive 18-inch alloy wheels, prominent side cladding, and bold black front and rear lower fascias. More functional bridge-type roof side rails are included, and a standard tow hitch makes it ready for action right from the dealership. Inside, the cabin gets a matching adventurous theme with durable H-Tex® trimmed seats and a sophisticated black headliner.

Limited Trim

The Limited trim represents the apex of the gasoline-powered Tucson lineup. It's designed for the driver who wants it all: the highest levels of technology, comfort, and sophistication, creating an experience that rivals many luxury-brand SUVs.

The Limited includes the features from the available SEL Convenience package (such as the panoramic sunroof and 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster) and elevates them further. The seats are trimmed in genuine leather, with the front seats offering both heating and ventilation, while rear-seat passengers enjoy heated seats of their own. Your soundtrack is provided by a rich Bose® premium audio system. The driving experience is enhanced by a suite of advanced technologies, including a full-color Head-Up Display (HUD), a Surround View Monitor, and the Blind-Spot View Monitor, which projects a live camera feed of your blind spot into the instrument cluster. The cabin is also noticeably quieter at highway speeds thanks to acoustic-laminated front side windows. Finally, the cockpit is modernized with a column-mounted electronic shift-by-wire system and steering-wheel-mounted paddle shifters, adding a touch of high-tech refinement.
