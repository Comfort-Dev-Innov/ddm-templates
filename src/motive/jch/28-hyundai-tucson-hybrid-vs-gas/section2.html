Powertrains of the Hyundai Tucson

Hybrid vs. Gas

Whether you prioritize proven simplicity or cutting-edge efficiency, Hyundai has engineered a powertrain that aligns with your needs. This choice will define your daily driving experience, from fuel station visits to the feeling you get when you press the accelerator.

The 2.5L Smartstream Gasoline Engine: Proven, Powerful, and Ready for Anything

The standard powertrain in the 2025 Tucson is a testament to refined and reliable engineering. It's a Smartstream 2.5-liter 4-cylinder engine that utilizes both Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) and Multi-Port Injection (MPI) to optimize performance and efficiency. This impressive engine produces a capable 187 horsepower and 178 lb-ft of torque, giving you the confident power you need for every driving situation.


This power is managed by a sophisticated 8-speed automatic transmission equipped with SHIFTRONIC®, which allows you to manually shift gears for a more engaging drive when you desire it. On the road, this translates to a smooth, predictable, and reassuring experience. It's the ideal companion for those long, steady cruises down I-40 or the Turner Turnpike, where the engine settles in comfortably, delivering power with quiet confidence. For drivers who appreciate straightforward mechanics and a history of proven reliability, the 2.5L engine is an outstanding choice. It's also a smart choice for city driving, as every gas model comes standard with Idle Stop & Go (ISG), a feature that automatically shuts off the engine when you're stopped to conserve fuel and seamlessly restarts it the moment you're ready to move.

The 1.6L Turbo Hybrid Powertrain: The Future of Efficiency

Choosing the Tucson Hybrid means stepping into a new era of performance. This advanced system pairs a spirited 1.6-liter turbocharged GDI 4-cylinder engine, which on its own produces 178 horsepower and a robust 195 lb-ft of torque, with a powerful 47.7 kW permanent magnet synchronous electric motor. The result of this synergy is a thrilling combined system output of 231 horsepower and an immense 271 lb-ft of torque. This power is channeled through a quick-shifting 6-speed automatic transmission specifically tuned for the hybrid system.


The hybrid's secret weapon is the instant torque delivered by the electric motor. When you're the first in line at a stoplight or need to merge decisively into the fast-moving traffic, that immediate response makes a world of difference. There's no lag, just a smooth, silent, and assertive surge of power. This characteristic makes the Tucson Hybrid exceptionally well-suited for the stop-and-go traffic that can build up around the OKC metro. The entire experience is quieter and more refined, giving the cabin a serene, near-luxury feel, especially during city driving.

Conquering Oklahoma Weather with HTRAC® All-Wheel Drive

Oklahoma weather is famously unpredictable. We're no strangers to sudden, intense thunderstorms that can make roads dangerously slick in an instant, or the treacherous black ice that can form on bridges and overpasses during a winter cold snap. For this reason, having a capable all-wheel-drive system is a crucial safety feature. While the gas-powered Tucson offers HTRAC® All-Wheel Drive as an option, it comes standard on every single Tucson Hybrid trim.


Hyundai's HTRAC® is far more than a basic AWD system. It's an intelligent, proactive technology that acts as your co-pilot in challenging conditions. The system's electronic brain monitors dozens of vehicle inputs—like wheel speed, throttle position, and steering angle—over 100 times per second. This allows it to anticipate a loss of traction and pre-emptively send power to the wheels with the most grip before you even feel a slip.


This is invaluable for maintaining stability during a sudden downpour or while navigating a slushy street after a winter storm.



The system adapts to your needs through Drive Mode Select, offering settings tailored for specific Oklahoma driving scenarios:

Normal, Smart, or Eco Mode: For your everyday commute from Moore to downtown OKC on dry roads, the system intelligently prioritizes sending power to the front wheels to maximize your fuel efficiency.

Sport Mode: Planning a scenic drive out to the Talimena Scenic Byway? Engaging Sport Mode sends more torque to the rear wheels (up to 50%), providing sharper handling and a more dynamic, rear-drive feel for an exhilarating experience on winding roads.

Snow Mode: Think of this as your Oklahoma Winter Confidence Plan. This mode is specifically engineered to give you maximum control on slick surfaces. It optimizes power distribution and adjusts throttle response to help you navigate icy bridges and overpasses. This mode gives you the peace of mind you need to get home safely.
