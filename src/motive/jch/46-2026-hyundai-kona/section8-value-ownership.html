Value Proposition and Ownership Costs

Kona's industry-leading warranty coverage includes:

  • New Vehicle Limited Warranty: 5 years / 60,000 miles
  • Powertrain Limited Warranty: 10 years / 100,000 miles
  • Complimentary scheduled maintenance for 3 years / 36,000 miles
  • 24/7 Hyundai Roadside Assistance is included for 5 years, ensuring coverage in emergencies.

In contrast, the Kia Niro offers similar warranty protection but does not include complimentary maintenance, which can increase ownership costs over time.

The Kona's streamlined 2026 trim changes, consolidating four trims into two, provide clear choices to match diverse budgets without sacrificing features.
