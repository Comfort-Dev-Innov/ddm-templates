Technology Features That Enhance Every Drive

The 2026 Kona is packed with technology to suit modern lifestyles. Standard across all trims is a vibrant 12.3-inch touchscreen that supports wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, enabling seamless smartphone integration without cluttering cabin space.

Higher trims introduce a fully digital 12.3-inch gauge cluster, ambient 64-color lighting, and an available Bose premium audio system that beats the Kia Niro's standard six-speaker setup and optional seven-speaker Harman/Kardon system in both clarity and immersive sound depth.

Additional conveniences such as NFC-based Hyundai Digital Key 2 Touch, wireless charging, and dual USB ports front and rear enable connectivity and power solutions tailored for busy commuters in Oklahoma City and surrounding metro areas.
