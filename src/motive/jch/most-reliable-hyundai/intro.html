Getting behind the wheel for the first time is a significant moment, whether you're 16 or 60. It represents freedom, responsibility, and the beginning of countless journeys ahead. The challenge is finding a car that balances practical needs with personal preferences. First-time drivers often want something that feels modern and connected, while those supporting them (family members, or the drivers themselves) prioritize safety, reliability, and smart financial decisions. The good news is these priorities don't have to compete.
