What is the Top Ford Model?

Ford has several standout models that have shaped its reputation and continue to dominate sales in Oklahoma and nationwide.

Ford F-Series

The Unrivaled Leader

No discussion of top Ford models is complete without the Ford F-Series.

This lineup has been America's best-selling vehicle for over four decades, with the F-150 model leading the charge. It boasts a 325-horsepower twin-turbo V6 engine starting point, with options scaling up to V8s and hybrid powertrains. Its towing capacity ranges from 8,200 to over 14,000 pounds depending on configuration.

The F-Series' blend of power, versatility, and technology, such as hands-free driving and advanced towing aids, make it perfect for both work and family life in Yukon and Mustang.

Ford Escape

Consistent Practicality

The Escape is a compact SUV known for its fuel efficiency and comfortable ride.

Newer models include hybrid and plug-in hybrid variants, providing excellent mileage without sacrificing performance.

Its advanced driver-assistance features are tailored for urban and suburban areas like Edmond and Moore.

Ford Mustang

Iconic Muscle and Style

The Mustang continues to be a symbol of American muscle with available engines up to 526 horsepower and sleek, aerodynamic designs.

It combines classic styling with modern technology, including advanced infotainment and driver assistance, making it a thrilling choice for Oklahoma drivers who want performance and style.
