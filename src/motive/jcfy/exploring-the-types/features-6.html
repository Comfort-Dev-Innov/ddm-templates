Discovering the Most Sporty Ford Models

For drivers in places like Bethany and Warr Acres craving performance and style, Ford's sportiest models deliver.

Ford Mustang

Available with a standard 3.7L V6 or an optional V8 engine, the Mustang offers up to 526 horsepower with a choice of manual or automatic transmissions. Its aerodynamic design and performance features make it a standout sporty car.

Ford Taurus SHO

Equipped with a 3.5L EcoBoost engine and all-wheel drive, the Taurus SHO combines power with advanced handling technologies like Torque Vectoring Control and Curve Control.

Ford Fusion Sport

This model features a 2.7L EcoBoost engine, intelligent all-wheel drive, and sport-tuned suspension, offering a sophisticated sporty driving experience.
