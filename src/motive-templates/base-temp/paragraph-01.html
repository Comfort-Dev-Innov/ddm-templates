Fuel Economy: Does the F-150 Hybrid Get Better Gas Mileage?

One of the most compelling reasons to consider the Ford F-150 Hybrid is its improved fuel economy. According to EPA estimates, the 2025 F-150 PowerBoost hybrid achieves approximately 22 mpg in the city and 24 mpg on the highway, combining for an overall rating of nearly 23 mpg. This is a marked improvement over the non-hybrid 3.5-liter EcoBoost V6, which averages 18-20 MPG combined.
