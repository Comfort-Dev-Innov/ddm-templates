Trim-by-Trim Breakdown

1
FOUNDATION OF INNOVATION
SE (Gas)
The SE defies the "base model" stereotype by offering an incredible array of standard features.
It comes with the 187-hp engine, the large 12.3-inch infotainment touchscreen, wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, and a robust suite of Hyundai SmartSense safety technologies, including Blind-Spot Collision Warning and Smart Cruise Control with Stop & Go—features that are often optional on competitors' base trims.
2
ENHANCED CONVENIENCE
SEL (Gas)
The SEL trim adds a host of desirable features that enhance daily convenience.
These include an 8-way power-adjustable driver's seat with lumbar support, heated front seats, a hands-free smart liftgate that opens automatically when the key is near, a wireless device charging pad, and the highly convenient Hyundai Digital Key 2, which allows you to use your smartphone as a key.
3
ADVENTURE READY
XRT (Gas)
The XRT is for those who love an adventurous look.
Built upon the SEL's feature set, the XRT adds a commanding presence with exclusive 18-inch black alloy wheels, rugged side cladding and lower fascias, blacked-out exterior trim, and functional bridge-type roof rails for mounting gear.
4
LUXURY REDEFINED
Limited (Gas)
The Limited trim transforms the Tucson into a legitimate rival for entry-level luxury SUVs. It is packed with premium amenities, including a large panoramic sunroof, supple leather-trimmed seats, ventilated front seats for cooling comfort in Oklahoma summers, heated rear seats, a rich-sounding Bose premium audio system, the 12.3-inch fully digital instrument cluster, a Surround View Monitor for 360-degree parking visibility, the innovative Blind-Spot View Monitor, and a 12-inch color Head-Up Display.

What's Included in the 2025 Tucson Limited Trim?

The Limited trim is a showcase of everything Hyundai can pack into a compact SUV. It's designed for the buyer who wants it all. The list of exclusive features is extensive and includes:

  • Premium Comfort: Leather-trimmed seating, ventilated front seats, heated rear seats, and a heated steering wheel.
  • Ambiance and Sound: A massive panoramic sunroof and a Bose® premium audio system.
  • Advanced Technology: The 12.3-inch digital instrument cluster, a 12-inch color Head-Up Display (HUD), and an Integrated Memory System for the driver's seat.
  • Ultimate Convenience and Safety: Remote Smart Parking Assist (RSPA), which allows you to park the vehicle remotely using the key fob; a Surround View Monitor (SVM) for a 360-degree bird's-eye view; the Blind-Spot View Monitor (BVM); and rain-sensing windshield wipers.
5
EFFICIENCY MEETS PERFORMANCE
Hybrid Trims (SEL Convenience, Limited)
The Hybrid models largely mirror the equipment levels of their gasoline counterparts but come standard with the powerful and efficient 231-hp hybrid powertrain and HTRAC All-Wheel Drive. The Blue trim is the efficiency champion, while the SEL Convenience adds the panoramic display and sunroof. The N Line offers a unique, aggressive appearance package with exclusive wheels, bumpers, and interior accents for a sport-inspired look, and the Limited Hybrid combines the best powertrain with all the premium features of the gas Limited.
