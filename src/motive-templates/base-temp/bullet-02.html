The 2025 Hyundai Tucson Trim Lineup

Hyundai has structured the 2025 Tucson lineup to provide a clear and logical progression of features, making it easy for you to find the perfect model for your needs and budget. The philosophy is straightforward:

  • the SE is the impressively well-equipped starting point.
  • The SEL adds a layer of popular comfort and convenience features.
  • The XRT delivers a bold, rugged aesthetic.
  • The motorsport-inspired N Line (available on Hybrid) provides a uniquely sporty look.
  • And the Limited offers a near-luxury experience packed with premium technology.
